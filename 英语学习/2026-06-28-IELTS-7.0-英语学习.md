## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”
**中文摘要**
这篇文章采访了一位有 30 年教龄的英语老师 Jen Roberts，讨论她如何把 AI 变成教学工具，而不是单纯视为作弊手段。她认为 AI 最有价值的地方在于反馈、评分和整理教学材料，能帮助老师更快地返回学生作业，也让修改更及时。她还强调，AI 不能替代教师判断，但可以作为“校准工具”，减少老师在疲劳状态下产生的评分偏差。文章也提到，教师应避免让学生直接使用不受控的通用 AI，并应优先使用能监控、保护隐私的教育类工具。整体来看，文章的核心观点是：AI 在教育中可以提高效率、公平性和可读性，但前提是使用方式要谨慎、透明且合规。

**核心词汇/短语**
1. `unlikely ally`｜意想不到的帮手｜原文：`has found an unlikely ally in AI`｜可替换：`unexpected helper`
2. `burnout`｜倦怠，精疲力竭｜原文：`the pandemic had pushed many educators into burnout`｜可替换：`exhaustion`
3. `time-constrained`｜时间受限的｜原文：`In my time-constrained world`｜可替换：`pressed for time`
4. `spot-on`｜非常准确的｜原文：`What the AI comes up with is usually spot-on`｜可替换：`accurate`
5. `version history`｜版本历史｜原文：`I can see version history`｜可替换：`document tracking`
6. `inappropriately`｜不恰当地｜原文：`use AI inappropriately`｜可替换：`improperly`
7. `balance check`｜校准检查；平衡核对｜原文：`I see it as my balance check`｜可替换：`reality check`
8. `mitigate`｜缓解，减轻｜原文：`AI can mitigate that issue`｜可替换：`reduce`

**阅读题目**
1. **True / False / Not Given**  
   Jen Roberts believes AI should replace teachers in marking essays.

2. **True / False / Not Given**  
   The article says students should use any AI tool they want as long as it is convenient.

3. **Matching Information**  
   Match the paragraph purpose to the correct idea:
   - A. AI helps reduce teacher fatigue and improve fairness
   - B. AI is mainly useful for lesson planning
   - C. AI should be used with privacy safeguards

   Paragraphs:
   1) Lines about grading and balance check  
   2) Lines about warnings on student data  
   3) Lines about prompts, feedback, and faster returns of work

4. **Multiple Choice**  
   Why does Jen Roberts like using AI for grading?
   - A. It makes her students depend on technology
   - B. It helps her compare judgments and improve consistency
   - C. It allows students to skip revision
   - D. It removes the need for human teachers

5. **Summary Completion**  
   Complete the summary with ONE WORD OR SHORT PHRASE from the article:
   AI is useful because it can give faster ________, help teachers create better materials, and act as a ________ against human bias.

**答案与解析**
1. **False**  
   文中明确说 AI 不是替代老师，而是帮助老师做反馈和校准。

2. **False**  
   文章强调要使用受控、合规的教育类工具，不是“任何 AI 都可以”。

3.  
   - 1 → A  
   - 2 → C  
   - 3 → B

4. **B**  
   她喜欢 AI 的原因是可以对照人类判断，减少疲劳带来的不一致。

5. **feedback / balance check**  
   合理答案应抓住“更快反馈”和“平衡检查”两个信息点。

**长难句拆解**
原句：`When generative AI emerged, she was quick to test whether it could make feedback faster and grading fairer.`

- 句子主干：`she was quick to test`
- 时间状语：`When generative AI emerged`
- 宾语从句：`whether it could make feedback faster and grading fairer`
- 逻辑关系：先交代 AI 出现的时间，再说明她立即进行验证，最后点出测试目标：提速和更公平。
- 可模仿表达：`When X emerged, I was quick to test whether it could make Y faster and Z better.`

2. Jonathan Haidt 认为问题的核心不只是“手机本身”，还包括什么？

**第二遍精听任务：听写句子**
请尽量逐句听写下面这些关键表达对应的原文内容：
1. Humans aren't just social — we're ultrasocial.
2. smartphones take over childhood
3. tablets replace textbooks
4. AI companies infiltrate our kids’ lives
5. three principles of technoskepticism
6. more concerned and hopeful than ever before
7. deep connection
8. wired like bees and ants
9. sounding the alarm
10. get it back

**易听错点提醒**
- 连读：`how it back`, `take over`, `more concerned and hopeful`
- 弱读：`and`, `to`, `of` 常被弱化
- 同音近音：`their/there`, `take/tech`, `screen/scream`
- 数字与时间：演讲年份、talk 的发布时间、对“two years after”等时间表达要特别注意
- 转折信号：`So`, `but`, `and yet`, `more than ever` 往往是观点转折点

**听后复述模板（80-120 词）**
Jonathan Haidt argues that smartphones and other digital devices are changing childhood in ways that affect attention, social connection, and emotional development. He says humans are deeply social creatures, but children now spend more time with screens and less time in face-to-face interaction. In his view, the problem is not only technology itself, but also how it reshapes habits, education, and relationships. He offers a technoskeptical perspective and suggests that people should think more carefully about how digital life affects young people. Overall, the talk is a warning, but also a call to respond with hope and action.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0
**Part 1**
1. Do you think teachers should use AI in class?
2. How often do you use technology for learning?
3. Do you think screen time affects children?

**Part 2 cue card**
Describe a time when technology helped you study or learn something.
You should say:
- what the technology was
- how you used it
- what problem it solved
- and explain whether it was helpful

**Part 3**
1. Should schools encourage AI use or restrict it?
2. How has technology changed the role of teachers?
3. Do you think students become more independent with digital tools?
4. What are the risks of relying too much on technology in education?

**每题作答思路**
- 普通答案思路：先直接回答，再举一个简单例子，再说原因。
- 7 分答案结构：`position → reason → example → qualification/contrast`。
- 可套用高分表达：
  - `From my perspective, ...`
  - `What matters more is not whether we use it, but how we use it.`
  - `It can be a double-edged sword.`
  - `On balance, I’d say ...`
  - `It helps, provided that ...`

**90 秒口语任务**
请大声回答：`Do you think AI should be used in schools?`
要求：
- 先表明立场
- 说 1 个优点
- 说 1 个风险
- 最后给出折中结论

**自评标准**
- `fluency`：是否停顿过多，能否连续说完
- `vocabulary`：是否用了 `double-edged sword`, `provided that`, `fairness` 之类表达
- `grammar`：时态、从句、比较结构是否稳定
- `pronunciation`：重音、连读、句子节奏是否自然
- `idea development`：是否有理由、例子和总结，而不是只给结论
