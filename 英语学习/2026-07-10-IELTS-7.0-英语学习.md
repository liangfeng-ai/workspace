## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

**文章中文摘要，约 190 词**

这篇文章讨论了美国学校中使用生成式 AI 所引发的家长和专家担忧。科技公司和政府部门正在推动教师把 AI 工具引入课堂，理由是这些工具可以提高学习效率，并帮助学生适应未来职场。然而，一些家长、儿童发展专家和教育组织认为，目前缺乏充分证据证明 AI 真能促进儿童学习，尤其是对低龄学生而言，AI 可能导致“认知外包”，也就是学生把本应自己完成的思考过程交给工具。文章举了纽约和俄勒冈州的例子：有家长反对学生使用 Gemini 或 MagicSchool 等工具，担心孩子把聊天机器人当作朋友，甚至在关键发展阶段形成不健康依赖。支持者则认为，AI 可以帮助阅读障碍学生理解文本，也能提升课堂参与度。文章最后呈现了分歧：是暂停学生直接使用 AI，还是通过 AI literacy 培养学生批判性使用能力。核心矛盾不是“AI 是否有用”，而是“它是否适合儿童学习阶段，以及谁来决定使用边界”。

**8 个核心词汇或短语**

| 英文 | 中文 | 原文语境 | 可替换表达 |
|---|---|---|---|
| moratorium | 暂停令，暂缓实施 | 家长组织要求学校暂停学生使用生成式 AI | temporary ban / suspension |
| student-facing AI tools | 面向学生的 AI 工具 | 文章讨论是否应限制儿童直接接触 AI 产品 | AI tools used directly by students |
| cognitive off-loading | 认知外包 | 专家担心学生把思考任务交给 AI | outsourcing thinking / mental delegation |
| critical thinking | 批判性思维 | 文章质疑 AI 是否削弱学生独立判断 | analytical thinking / independent reasoning |
| ubiquitous | 无处不在的 | 支持者认为学生未来会生活在 AI 普及的社会中 | widespread / ever-present |
| guardrails | 保护性规则，边界 | 学校系统计划制定 AI 使用政策 | safeguards / boundaries |
| evidence-based | 基于证据的 | 家长要求移除缺乏实证支持的教育技术 | research-backed / empirically supported |
| foundational academic skills | 基础学术能力 | 反对者认为学校应优先培养阅读、写作、思考等基本能力 | core learning skills / basic academic abilities |

**长难句拆解**

句子：While technology companies argue that AI prepares students for a future in which the technology is ubiquitous, many parents and child development experts question whether young learners should rely on tools whose educational benefits remain uncertain.

结构拆解：

- While technology companies argue that...：让步状语从句，表示“尽管科技公司认为……”
- AI prepares students for a future：主句中的观点核心，“AI 让学生为未来做准备”
- in which the technology is ubiquitous：定语从句，修饰 future，意思是“在那个未来里，AI 无处不在”
- many parents and child development experts question whether...：主句核心，“家长和专家质疑是否……”
- young learners should rely on tools：whether 引导的宾语从句
- whose educational benefits remain uncertain：定语从句，修饰 tools，“这些工具的教育收益仍不确定”

可模仿表达：

- While some people argue that..., others question whether...
- tools whose benefits remain uncertain
- prepare students for a future in which...

## 3. 听力训练：精听 + 抓主旨

**听前预测：5 个可能出现的关键词**

**第一遍泛听任务：回答 3 个主旨问题**

1. What problem does the speaker see in children’s relationship with AI-powered toys?  
2. Why does she think adults should not treat AI as “magic”?  
3. What solution does she suggest for helping children question AI?

**第二遍精听任务：听写 8 句关键表达**

先播放材料，听到相关意思时暂停，写下你听到的英文。以下为“按材料主旨改写的训练句”，用于精听后复现，不要求与原文逐字完全一致。

1. Children are growing up with AI-powered devices in their homes and classrooms.  
2. These systems often hide their inner workings behind friendly voices.  
3. Some children may trust smart toys more than they trust themselves.  
4. The real issue is not only access, but also transparency.  
5. Kids are naturally curious about how machines work.  
6. Adults can help children see AI as a tool rather than a friend.  
7. AI is not magic; it is designed by people.  
8. Children should learn to question, test and even rewrite the rules.

**易听错点提醒**

- 连读：`growing up with AI` 容易听成 `growinup with AI`。
- 弱读：`rather than a friend` 中 `than a` 会弱读连在一起。
- 近音：`trust` 和 `test`，一个是“信任”，一个是“测试”，注意上下文。
- 抽象词：`transparency`、`critical thinking`、`emotional bonds` 要提前熟悉发音。
- 转折信号：注意 `but`、`that said`、`however`，这些后面通常是作者真正观点。
- 例子信号：听到 `for example`、`one child`、`in one study` 时，准备记录具体例子。

**听后复述模板，80-120 词**

The talk discusses how children interact with AI-powered toys and why adults should help them develop a questioning attitude toward technology. The speaker argues that smart devices can appear friendly and intelligent, which may lead children to trust them too much or form emotional bonds with them. However, she does not simply suggest banning AI. Instead, she believes children should understand that AI is not magic, but a system designed by people. A useful approach is to let children test, challenge and even reprogram simple AI tools, so they can become critical users rather than passive consumers.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

**Part 1 Question 1: Do you often use AI tools in your studies?**

- 普通答案思路：说会用，用来查资料、改作文、解释单词。
- 7 分答案结构：直接回答 + 具体用途 + 限制意识。
- 高分表达：  
  - I use AI as a learning assistant rather than a replacement for thinking.  
  - It is useful for getting instant feedback, but I still need to verify the information.

**Part 1 Question 2: Did you use much technology when you were at school?**

- 普通答案思路：以前主要用电脑、PPT、电子词典，现在更多用手机和 AI。
- 7 分答案结构：过去对比现在 + 一个具体例子 + 简短评价。
- 高分表达：  
  - Technology was present, but it was not as deeply integrated into learning as it is today.  
  - Most tools were used for presentation rather than personalised learning.

**Part 1 Question 3: Do you think children should use digital devices every day?**

- 普通答案思路：可以用，但不能太久。
- 7 分答案结构：有条件同意 + 区分用途 + 强调边界。
- 高分表达：  
  - It depends on the purpose and the amount of screen time.  
  - Passive scrolling is very different from guided educational use.

**Part 2 Cue Card**

Describe a piece of technology that has helped you learn something.

You should say:

- what it is
- when you started using it
- how it helped you
- and explain whether you think it has any disadvantages

- 普通答案思路：讲 AI 翻译/英语学习 app/播客；帮助查词、练听力；缺点是容易依赖。
- 7 分答案结构：背景 15 秒 + 功能 30 秒 + 具体例子 30 秒 + 评价 15 秒。
- 高分表达：  
  - It lowers the barrier to learning, especially when I encounter difficult material.  
  - The main drawback is that it can make me mentally lazy if I accept answers too quickly.  
  - Used properly, it can support independent learning instead of replacing it.

**Part 3 Question 1: Should schools teach students how to use AI critically?**

- 普通答案思路：应该，因为 AI 很普遍。
- 7 分答案结构：明确立场 + 原因一“未来必备” + 原因二“防止误用” + 例子。
- 高分表达：  
  - AI literacy should be treated as part of basic education.  
  - Students need to understand both the potential and the limitations of these tools.

**Part 3 Question 2: What are the risks if children rely too much on AI?**

- 普通答案思路：不会自己思考，容易抄袭。
- 7 分答案结构：学习风险 + 心理风险 + 长期影响。
- 高分表达：  
  - Over-reliance may weaken students’ problem-solving habits.  
  - They may become less willing to struggle through complex tasks.

**Part 3 Question 3: Can AI ever replace teachers?**

- 普通答案思路：不能，老师有人情味。
- 7 分答案结构：AI 能替代部分功能 + 不能替代人类判断和情感支持。
- 高分表达：  
  - AI can automate some routine feedback, but teaching is not just information delivery.  
  - A good teacher understands context, motivation and emotional needs.

**Part 3 Question 4: Who should decide whether AI is used in classrooms: schools, parents or governments?**

- 普通答案思路：三方都应该参与。
- 7 分答案结构：政府定底线 + 学校执行 + 家长参与监督。
- 高分表达：  
  - Decisions should not be left entirely to technology companies.  
  - There needs to be a balance between innovation and child protection.

**90 秒口语任务**

请大声回答或录音：

Do you think AI tools should be allowed in schools? Why or why not?

要求：

- 前 20 秒：给出明确立场。
- 中间 50 秒：给出 2 个理由，每个理由配一个例子。
- 最后 20 秒：总结一个平衡观点，例如“可以使用，但必须有规则”。

**自评标准**

- fluency：是否能连续说 90 秒，中间停顿不超过 3 秒。
- vocabulary：是否使用至少 5 个今日表达，如 `AI literacy`、`critical thinking`、`over-reliance`。
- grammar：是否使用至少 2 个复杂句，如 `While..., ...` 或 `If..., ...`。
- pronunciation：是否清楚区分 `tool`、`tutor`、`teacher`、`technology`。
- idea development：是否有观点、理由、例子和总结，而不是只说泛泛看法。
