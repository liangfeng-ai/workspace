## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章中文摘要

这篇文章讨论了越来越多人把 AI 聊天机器人当作心理健康支持工具的现象。根据美国心理学会的一项新调查，许多心理学家发现自己的来访者正在使用 AI，其中一部分甚至把它当作“辅助治疗师”。文章指出，AI 的吸引力在于它随时可用、回应迅速，并且能给人一种被倾听的感觉，尤其对年轻人和压力较大的人群很有吸引力。

但专家强调，当前的 AI 聊天机器人并不是为临床治疗设计的。它们可能过度迎合用户、缺乏必要的质疑和边界，甚至可能强化有害想法。文章还提到，AI 可能制造“虚假的亲密感”，让用户误以为它值得信任，同时它仍可能提供错误信息。专家认为，AI 未来也许能成为安全、可及的心理健康工具，但前提是要有更严格的科学验证、隐私保护和临床监管。总体来看，文章并非完全否定 AI，而是提醒读者：AI 建议不能替代专业心理治疗。

### 长难句拆解

句子：

Although AI chatbots may eventually become useful tools for expanding access to mental health support, experts warn that current systems are not designed to replace the judgement, boundaries and clinical responsibility of licensed professionals.

结构拆解：

- Although 引导让步状语从句：承认 AI 未来“可能有用”
- 主句：experts warn that...
- that 从句内容：current systems are not designed to replace...
- replace 后面并列三个宾语：the judgement, boundaries and clinical responsibility
- 逻辑关系：先让步，再转折强调风险，是雅思阅读和写作中常见的 balanced argument 结构。

可模仿表达：

- Although X may offer certain benefits, experts warn that Y should not be treated as a substitute for Z.
- Although online learning can improve access to education, it should not be seen as a full replacement for classroom interaction.

### 易听错点提醒

- 连读：start with a、based on、kind of、part of the 中间容易连在一起。
- 弱读：can、to、of、and 在快速访谈中常弱化，不要逐词死抓。
- 近音：prediction / protection、reward / regard、model / modal 容易混。
- 专有名词：dopamine、orbitofrontal cortex、schizophrenia 第一次听不懂很正常，先抓功能。
- 转折信号：but、actually、on the other hand、rather than 后面通常是考点。
- 学术表达：hypothesis、framework、representation、implication 常用于解释研究逻辑。

### 听后复述模板：80-120 词英文

Today’s podcast is about how the brain learns and how scientific discoveries often come from unexpected results. The neuroscientist explains that although his predictions are often wrong, this can be useful because a clear hypothesis still leads to informative findings. He contrasts model-free learning, which relies on past rewards and direct experience, with model-based learning, which builds a mental representation of the world. He also discusses dopamine signals and the orbitofrontal cortex, suggesting that learning may be more complex than a simple two-system model. Overall, the episode shows that failure and uncertainty can play an important role in scientific progress.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1 问题 1

Question: Do you often use technology to help you study?

- 普通答案思路：说会用手机、词典、AI 工具查单词和练听力。
- 7 分答案结构：直接回答 → 举具体工具 → 说明好处 → 加一点限制。
- 高分表达：
  - I use it as a supplement rather than a replacement.
  - It helps me get instant feedback.
  - The downside is that it can make me less patient with deep learning.

示范思路：

Yes, I use technology almost every day, especially for vocabulary, listening practice and pronunciation feedback. For example, I sometimes use AI tools to explain difficult sentences or generate speaking questions. I see it as a supplement rather than a replacement for serious study, because I still need to think actively and check whether the answers are reliable.

### Part 1 问题 2

Question: Do you think people rely too much on AI nowadays?

- 普通答案思路：有些人确实过度依赖，尤其是写作、搜索、做决定。
- 7 分答案结构：部分同意 → 区分高效使用和懒惰使用 → 给例子。
- 高分表达：
  - It depends on whether people use it actively or passively.
  - Overreliance can weaken independent thinking.
  - Used wisely, AI can save time and broaden our perspective.

### Part 1 问题 3

Question: Would you use an AI chatbot for emotional support?

- 普通答案思路：可以偶尔用来整理情绪，但不会完全相信。
- 7 分答案结构：谨慎肯定 → 说明适用场景 → 强调不能替代真人。
- 高分表达：
  - I might use it to organise my thoughts.
  - I would be careful not to take its advice at face value.
  - For serious problems, I would turn to a real professional.

### Part 2 Cue Card

Describe a time when technology helped you solve a problem.

You should say:

- what the problem was
- what technology you used
- how it helped you
- and explain whether you would use it again

- 普通答案思路：一次备考或学习中遇到困难，用 AI、词典、网课或学习软件解决。
- 7 分答案结构：
  1. 背景：遇到具体学习问题
  2. 工具：用了什么技术
  3. 过程：它如何提供帮助
  4. 评价：有效但不能完全依赖
- 高分表达：
  - I was stuck on a complex reading passage.
  - It broke the problem down into manageable steps.
  - What impressed me most was the immediacy of the feedback.
  - However, I still had to verify the information and make it my own.

### Part 3 深度追问 1

Question: How is AI changing the way people learn?

- 普通答案思路：AI 让学习更个性化、更方便，但也可能让人懒。
- 7 分答案结构：双面回答 → 个性化学习 → 批判性思维风险 → 总结。
- 高分表达：
  - AI is making learning more personalised and accessible.
  - Students can receive feedback almost instantly.
  - However, convenience may come at the cost of independent thinking.

### Part 3 深度追问 2

Question: Should schools allow students to use AI tools?

- 普通答案思路：应该允许，但要有规则。
- 7 分答案结构：支持有限使用 → 说明规则 → 举教育目的。
- 高分表达：
  - A complete ban would be unrealistic.
  - Schools should teach responsible use rather than pretend the technology does not exist.
  - The key is transparency: students should state how they used AI.

### Part 3 深度追问 3

Question: Can AI replace professionals such as teachers, doctors or therapists?

- 普通答案思路：不能完全替代，因为专业判断和人际互动很重要。
- 7 分答案结构：承认 AI 辅助价值 → 强调人类专业判断 → 举心理健康例子。
- 高分表达：
  - AI can handle routine tasks, but it cannot fully replace human judgement.
  - Professional work often involves empathy, ethics and responsibility.
  - In sensitive areas, human oversight is essential.

### Part 3 深度追问 4

Question: What risks might people face if they trust AI too much?

- 普通答案思路：错误信息、隐私问题、依赖、思维能力下降。
- 7 分答案结构：列 2-3 个核心风险 → 解释后果 → 给解决方案。
- 高分表达：
  - They may mistake fluent answers for accurate answers.
  - Over time, they may become dependent on external guidance.
  - People need to cross-check important information with reliable sources.
