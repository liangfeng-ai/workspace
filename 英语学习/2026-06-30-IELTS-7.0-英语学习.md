## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”
基于今日阅读材料，生成：
- 文章摘要：这篇文章讨论了一位资深教师如何把 AI 正确引入课堂。作者强调，AI 不是用来替代学习过程，而是帮助学生进行草稿修改、思路整理和个性化练习。文章也提到，教师需要明确规则，防止学生过度依赖 AI，尤其是在写作和作业环节。整体观点是：教育中的 AI 应该服务于学习目标，而不是削弱学生的独立思考能力。  
- 核心词汇/短语：
  1. `educational technology` — 教育技术  
     原文语境：用于描述 AI 进入课堂的背景  
     可替换表达：`edtech`
  2. `the right way` — 正确方式  
     原文语境：教师如何恰当地使用 AI  
     可替换表达：`properly`
  3. `classroom` — 课堂  
     原文语境：AI 被应用的场景  
     可替换表达：`learning environment`
  4. `personalized learning` — 个性化学习  
     原文语境：AI 帮助学生按需练习  
     可替换表达：`tailored learning`
  5. `depend on` — 依赖  
     原文语境：学生可能过度依赖 AI  
     可替换表达：`rely on`
  6. `draft` — 草稿  
     原文语境：AI 可帮助修改作文初稿  
     可替换表达：`rough version`
  7. `independent thinking` — 独立思考  
     原文语境：作者担心 AI 影响学习能力  
     可替换表达：`critical thinking`
  8. `guidelines` — 指导原则 / 规则  
     原文语境：教师需要制定使用规范  
     可替换表达：`rules`

- 阅读题练习：
  1. True/False/Not Given：  
     The teacher believes AI should replace all homework.  
  2. True/False/Not Given：  
     Clear classroom rules are necessary when students use AI.  
  3. Multiple Choice：  
     What is the teacher’s main concern?  
     A. AI is too expensive for schools  
     B. Students may stop thinking independently  
     C. Teachers cannot learn to use AI  
     D. AI cannot improve writing  
  4. Matching：匹配观点与内容  
     - A. AI as a helper  
     - B. AI as a risk  
     - C. AI as a learning tool  
     - 1. Supports drafting and revision  
     - 2. May reduce student effort  
     - 3. Adapts practice to student needs  
  5. Summary Completion：  
     Complete the summary with one word each:  
     AI can support students in writing and revision, but teachers should set clear __________ to prevent overuse and protect __________ thinking.

- 答案与解析：
  1. False — 文章强调 AI 服务学习，不是替代所有作业。  
  2. True — 文章核心之一就是需要明确规则。  
  3. B — 作者最担心的是学生失去独立思考。  
  4. A-1, B-2, C-3  
  5. `guidelines`; `independent` — 分别对应“规则”和“独立思考”。

- 长难句拆解：
  - 句子：`AI can support students in writing and revision, but teachers should set clear guidelines to prevent overuse and protect independent thinking.`
  - 结构：前半句讲 AI 的积极作用，后半句用 `but` 转折，强调教师需要设规则。  
  - 逻辑关系：`support` 与 `prevent` 形成对比；`to prevent... and protect...` 是目的状语。  
  - 可模仿表达：`X can help with..., but we should set clear rules to avoid... and preserve...`

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0
围绕今日主题，生成：
- Part 1 问题：
  1. Do you often use AI tools for studying?
  2. Do you think technology helps students learn better?
  3. Should children use AI in school?
- Part 2 cue card：  
  Describe a time when you used technology to help you learn something. You should say:
  - what the technology was
  - what you learned
  - how it helped you
  - and explain whether you would use it again

- Part 3 深度追问：
  1. How should schools control students’ use of AI?
  2. What are the risks of relying too much on technology?
  3. Can technology improve creativity, or does it reduce it?
  4. How will education change in the next ten years?

- 每题答案思路 + 7分结构 + 高分表达：
  - Part 1 Q1  
    普通思路：说你会偶尔用 AI 查词、改写、找思路。  
    7分结构：`Yes/No + example + caution + result`  
    高分表达：`I use it as a support tool rather than a shortcut.`
  - Part 1 Q2  
    普通思路：技术有帮助，但要看怎么用。  
    7分结构：`balanced opinion + specific use + limitation`  
    高分表达：`Technology is helpful, provided that it is used purposefully.`
  - Part 1 Q3  
    普通思路：儿童可以用，但要有监督。  
    7分结构：`position + reason + condition`  
    高分表达：`It can be useful as long as it encourages curiosity instead of passive dependence.`
  - Part 2  
    普通思路：讲一个英语 app、字幕、网课或词典工具。  
    7分结构：`when/what → problem → how it helped → reflection`  
    高分表达：`It gave me immediate feedback, which made my learning much more efficient.`
  - Part 3 Q1  
    普通思路：学校要设规则，防止作弊。  
    7分结构：`policy + monitoring + teaching purpose`  
    高分表达：`Schools should regulate usage without eliminating the educational value of AI.`
  - Part 3 Q2  
    普通思路：懒惰、依赖、思考能力下降。  
    7分结构：`risk 1 + risk 2 + consequence`  
    高分表达：`Overreliance can weaken problem-solving skills over time.`
  - Part 3 Q3  
    普通思路：两者都有可能。  
    7分结构：`nuanced view + example + conclusion`  
    高分表达：`Used well, it can actually free up mental space for more original thinking.`
  - Part 3 Q4  
    普通思路：未来会更多线上化、AI 化。  
    7分结构：`trend + impact + warning`  
    高分表达：`The biggest change will be the shift from memorisation to guidance and critical thinking.`
