## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章中文摘要

这篇评论文章围绕前沿 AI 的潜在风险展开，核心观点是：如果社会等到灾难发生后才监管 AI，代价可能过高。作者提到，一些 AI 公司正在接近能够自我改进、自动执行复杂任务的阶段，这可能让网络攻击、自动化决策和社会失控风险迅速扩大。文章并不只是反对 AI，而是批评当前科技公司和政府在监管上的迟缓与矛盾：一方面企业强调 AI 的巨大潜力，另一方面却承认其可能带来严重后果。作者认为，AI 安全不应依赖企业自愿约束，也不能只在事故发生后补救，而需要更早、更强、更透明的治理框架。对雅思阅读来说，这篇文章特别适合训练识别作者态度、理解因果链条、区分事实与评论，以及积累科技监管类表达。

### 8 个核心词汇或短语

| 英文 | 中文 | 原文语境 | 可替换表达 |
|---|---|---|---|
| regulate AI | 监管人工智能 | 文章讨论是否需要更早、更严格地管理前沿 AI | govern AI / oversee AI |
| recursive self-improvement | 递归式自我改进 | 指 AI 系统不断提升自身能力的过程 | self-enhancing loop / iterative self-improvement |
| runaway feedback loop | 失控的反馈循环 | 用来描述技术能力不断加速、难以控制的风险 | uncontrollable cycle / escalating loop |
| irreversible loss of control | 不可逆的失控 | 指人类可能无法重新掌控系统 | permanent loss of oversight |
| cyber-attacks | 网络攻击 | 文章担心 AI 可自动执行复杂网络攻击 | digital attacks / cyber operations |
| frontier AI development | 前沿 AI 开发 | 指最先进、能力最强的 AI 系统研发 | cutting-edge AI research |
| human assistance | 人类协助 | 文章提到某些系统可能无需人类帮助完成任务 | human intervention / manual support |
| countermeasures | 应对措施；反制手段 | 面对 AI 风险，需要提前建立安全措施 | safeguards / protective measures |

#### Question 5: Summary Completion

Complete the summary using **ONE WORD OR PHRASE** from the vocabulary list above.

The article argues that society needs stronger ways to __________ before advanced systems create risks that are difficult to reverse.

### 答案与解析

1. **False**  
   解析：作者并不是说 AI 毫无价值，也没有主张完全禁止 AI，而是强调需要监管和安全措施。

2. **True**  
   解析：标题和文章论点都指向同一观点：不能等到类似重大灾难发生后才行动。

3. **C**  
   解析：recursive self-improvement 的核心风险在于能力提升可能形成加速循环，导致人类难以控制。

4. **B**  
   解析：文章语言带有明显警示性，强调风险、监管滞后和可能的严重后果。

5. **regulate AI**  
   解析：句子意思是“社会需要更强的方式来监管 AI”，与文章主旨一致。

### 长难句拆解

**句子**：If an AI system becomes capable of improving its own abilities faster than humans can evaluate or control them, society may face a form of technological risk that traditional regulation is too slow to address.

- **主句**：society may face a form of technological risk  
  社会可能面对一种技术风险。
- **条件状语从句**：If an AI system becomes capable of improving its own abilities  
  如果 AI 系统能够提升自身能力。
- **比较结构**：faster than humans can evaluate or control them  
  比人类评估或控制它们的速度更快。
- **定语从句**：that traditional regulation is too slow to address  
  传统监管机制来不及处理的风险。
- **逻辑关系**：条件 → 后果 → 风险性质。
- **可模仿表达**：If X becomes capable of doing Y faster than humans can respond, society may face a risk that existing systems are too slow to address.

### 易听错点提醒

- **连读**：what happens when / wired like / take over 容易连成一串。  
- **弱读**：are, to, of, and 在 TED 演讲中常被弱化。  
- **近音词**：social / societal，screen / scheme，principle / principal。  
- **转折信号**：but, yet, however, at the same time 后面通常是核心观点。  
- **抽象名词**：connection, anxiety, skepticism, principle 听到后要立刻判断论证方向。  
- **并列结构**：smartphones, tablets and AI companies 这类三项并列容易漏掉中间项。

### 听后复述模板：80-120 词英文

Jonathan Haidt argues that we should become more skeptical about the role of technology in modern life, especially in childhood. He does not simply reject technology, but he warns that smartphones, tablets and AI tools may weaken children’s social development if they are introduced without limits. His main point is that human beings need deep social connection, not only efficient digital interaction. Therefore, schools, parents and technology companies should think more carefully about how digital tools shape attention, relationships and mental health. Overall, the talk encourages a balanced attitude: we can use technology, but we should not allow it to redesign childhood without public debate.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1 Question 1

**Question**：Do you often use AI tools in your studies or work?

- **普通答案思路**：说自己会用 AI 查资料、改作文、练口语。
- **7 分答案结构**：直接回答 → 具体用途 → 优点 → 小限制。
- **高分表达**：
  - I use AI mainly as a learning assistant rather than a replacement for thinking.
  - It helps me organise ideas more efficiently.
  - I still try to verify important information myself.

### Part 1 Question 2

**Question**：Do you think people spend too much time on smartphones?

- **普通答案思路**：是的，很多人刷短视频，影响注意力。
- **7 分答案结构**：总体判断 → 举例 → 影响 → 个人做法。
- **高分表达**：
  - Smartphones are useful, but they can easily become attention traps.
  - Many people check their phones almost automatically.
  - I try to set boundaries, especially when I study.

### Part 1 Question 3

**Question**：Should children be allowed to use digital devices at school?

- **普通答案思路**：可以用，但要限制。
- **7 分答案结构**：平衡观点 → 学习好处 → 潜在风险 → 条件。
- **高分表达**：
  - Digital devices can support learning if they are used with a clear purpose.
  - Without supervision, they may distract students from deep learning.
  - The key is not access, but responsible use.

### Part 2 Cue Card

**Describe a piece of technology that has changed the way people learn.**

You should say:

- what it is
- how people use it
- what benefits it brings
- and explain whether you think it has any disadvantages

**普通答案思路**：讲 AI 工具或智能手机，帮助查资料、练英语，但也让人依赖。

**7 分答案结构**：

1. 开头点题：选择 AI learning tools。  
2. 解释使用方式：查资料、生成例句、模拟口语考试。  
3. 说明优点：效率高、个性化、即时反馈。  
4. 转折谈缺点：可能削弱独立思考。  
5. 总结观点：工具有价值，但需要自律。

**高分表达**：

- It has made learning more personalised and accessible.
- The biggest advantage is instant feedback.
- However, over-reliance on it may weaken critical thinking.
- I see it as a tool to extend my ability, not replace it.

### Part 3 Question 1

**Question**：How might AI change education in the next ten years?

- **普通答案思路**：AI 会让学习更个性化，老师角色改变。
- **7 分答案结构**：趋势 → 例子 → 影响 → 风险。
- **高分表达**：
  - AI is likely to make education more adaptive.
  - Teachers may spend less time delivering information and more time guiding students.
  - However, schools must prevent technology from widening inequality.

### Part 3 Question 2

**Question**：Should governments regulate powerful AI systems?

- **普通答案思路**：应该，因为风险大。
- **7 分答案结构**：明确立场 → 原因 1 安全 → 原因 2 公平 → 让步。
- **高分表达**：
  - Powerful technologies should not be governed only by private companies.
  - Regulation can create accountability and public trust.
  - The challenge is to regulate innovation without killing it.

### Part 3 Question 3

**Question**：Why are people often attracted to new technologies even when they know the risks?

- **普通答案思路**：因为方便、新鲜、能提高效率。
- **7 分答案结构**：心理原因 → 社会原因 → 商业设计 → 总结。
- **高分表达**：
  - Convenience often outweighs long-term concerns.
  - New technology creates a sense of progress and social status.
  - Many platforms are deliberately designed to be addictive.

### Part 3 Question 4

**Question**：Do you think technology makes people more connected or more isolated?

- **普通答案思路**：两方面都有。
- **7 分答案结构**：双边观点 → 在线连接 → 线下削弱 → 条件结论。
- **高分表达**：
  - It depends on whether technology complements or replaces real interaction.
  - Online communication can maintain relationships across distance.
  - But if it replaces face-to-face contact, it may deepen loneliness.
