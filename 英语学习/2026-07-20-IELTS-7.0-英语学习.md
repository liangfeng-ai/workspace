## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

**中文摘要，约 180 词**

这篇文章讨论数字环境如何影响儿童和青少年的健康。作者认为，社交媒体、网络游戏和生成式 AI 既能带来教育、医疗、沟通和包容性方面的机会，也可能对儿童的心理健康、睡眠、隐私和现实社交造成风险。文章强调，问题不在于简单地赞美或否定技术，而在于认识到平台设计、算法推荐、商业模式和监管制度会共同塑造儿童的成长环境。许多国家已经开始限制未成年人使用社交媒体，或推动年龄验证、平台责任和安全设计。文章还指出，过度数字暴露可能与焦虑、抑郁、睡眠不足、孤独和久坐行为有关，AI 生成内容和深度伪造也增加了新的安全风险。作者主张采取预防性原则，同时保留技术用于教育、健康和无障碍支持的积极作用。最终，政府、企业、学校、家庭、公共卫生机构和青少年本人都应参与制定更健康、更透明、更负责任的数字环境。

**8 个核心词汇或短语**

| 英文 | 中文 | 原文语境 | 可替换表达 |
|---|---|---|---|
| digital environments | 数字环境 | 指社交媒体、游戏、AI 等共同构成的成长空间 | online ecosystems / digital spaces |
| determinants of health | 健康决定因素 | 技术环境会影响身心健康 | factors shaping health |
| far-reaching benefits | 深远好处 | 数字工具可支持教育、医疗和沟通 | wide-ranging advantages |
| grave risks | 严重风险 | 对儿童发展、隐私和安全的潜在危害 | serious threats / major dangers |

| precautionary approach | 预防性方法 | 在长期影响不明时先保护儿童 | risk-based approach / preventive stance |

**Question 5: Summary Completion**

Complete the summary with ONE phrase from the box.

Box:  
**public-health imperative / entertainment habit / copy-and-paste model / commercial strategy**

The article suggests that protecting children online is not merely a family issue but a __________________, because digital design, regulation and business incentives can influence children’s mental and physical well-being.

**答案与解析**

1. **False**  
   文章不是说彻底移除技术，而是强调要最大化好处、减少危害。注意 IELTS 中 “all / always / completely” 常常是陷阱。

2. **True**  
   文章提到多个国家正在推进未成年人社交媒体限制、年龄验证或类似保护措施。

3. **B**  
   文章核心担忧是算法可能为了增加注意力和停留时间，而不是为了信息准确性来过滤内容。

4. **C**  
   作者承认 AI 可用于教育、无障碍和健康支持，但长期影响仍不清楚，因此支持谨慎监管。

5. **public-health imperative**  
   这里强调儿童线上保护已经上升为公共健康问题，而非单纯的个人选择。

**长难句拆解**

改写自文章核心观点的长难句：

Although digital tools can expand children’s access to learning, healthcare and social connection, their benefits depend heavily on how platforms are designed, governed and monetized.

- 主干：their benefits depend heavily on...
- 让步从句：Although digital tools can expand...
- 并列宾语：learning, healthcare and social connection
- 三个并列被动结构：designed, governed and monetized
- 逻辑关系：先承认技术有好处，再指出这些好处不是自动发生的，而取决于设计、治理和商业模式。
- 可模仿表达：  
  **Although X can bring benefits, its impact depends heavily on how it is designed, regulated and used.**

**第二遍精听任务：听写 8 句关键表达**

请播放材料，暂停听写。先不要看 transcript。

1. Cycling is a popular activity in Britain.
2. Many people choose cycling because it’s good for their health and the environment.
3. Electric bikes are making cycling accessible to more people, including older people.
4. The bicycle is deeply ingrained into Dutch culture.
5. The rise of cycling started in the industrial era.
6. This was a really important milestone for cycling in the Netherlands.
7. Gulf cities like Dubai want a reputation as places with a good quality of life.
8. It’s certainly not a copy and paste of what is good in Holland or Denmark.

**易听错点提醒**

- 连读：`good for their health` 容易听成 `good fuh their health`
- 弱读：`because it’s` 中 `it’s` 很轻，注意不要漏掉
- 近音：`cycling` / `sickling`，注意第一个音节是 /saɪ/
- 数字：`95,000 miles`、`24 million bikes`，注意单位和数量
- 地名：`the Netherlands`、`Holland`、`the UAE`、`Dubai`、`Abu Dhabi`
- 转折信号：`Of course`、`But`、`However` 后面通常是答案重点

**听后复述模板，80-120 词**

Today’s programme discusses whether people should cycle more and compares cycling cultures in different countries. It explains that cycling is becoming more accessible because of better cycle tracks and electric bikes. The Netherlands is presented as a country where cycling is deeply ingrained in everyday life, partly because bicycle manufacturing took off in the industrial era. The programme also looks at the UAE, where cities such as Dubai hope cycling can improve quality of life and liveability. However, Dubai cannot simply copy the Dutch model because the climate and urban conditions are different. Overall, cycling can be a greener and healthier option, but it needs local planning.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

**Part 1 Question 1: Do you often use digital devices?**

- 普通答案思路：每天用手机和电脑，学习、工作、娱乐都离不开。
- 7 分答案结构：频率 + 主要用途 + 控制方式 + 小评价。
- 高分表达：
  - I rely on digital devices on a daily basis.
  - They are useful, but they can easily become a distraction.
  - I try to set boundaries around my screen time.

**示范回答**

Yes, I use digital devices every day, mainly for studying, reading news and keeping in touch with friends. I would say they make my life much more efficient, but they can also become a source of distraction if I don’t control my screen time. So I try to use them with a clear purpose rather than just scrolling endlessly.

**Part 1 Question 2: Do people in your city like cycling?**

- 普通答案思路：有人喜欢，但不是主流，受道路和安全影响。
- 7 分答案结构：整体情况 + 原因 + 个人观察。
- 高分表达：
  - Cycling is gaining popularity, but it is still not the default choice.
  - Safety concerns put many people off.
  - Better infrastructure would make a real difference.

**Part 1 Question 3: How do you usually get health information?**

- 普通答案思路：网络、医生、公众号、视频。
- 7 分答案结构：来源 + 判断标准 + 风险意识。
- 高分表达：
  - I try to cross-check information from different sources.
  - Not everything online is evidence-based.
  - I’m cautious about sensational health claims.

**Part 2 Cue Card**

Describe a technology or lifestyle habit that has influenced people’s health.

You should say:

- what it is
- how people use it
- what benefits it brings
- what risks it may have

And explain whether you think it is mostly positive or negative.

**Part 2 7 分答案结构**

1. 开头直接点题：I’d like to talk about...
2. 解释使用场景：People use it to...
3. 平衡优缺点：On the positive side... However...
4. 给出个人判断：Overall, I would say...

**可套用高分表达**

- It has become deeply ingrained in modern life.
- The benefits are real, but they are not automatic.
- It depends on how responsibly people use it.
- It can improve access to information, but it may also affect mental well-being.
- I wouldn’t say it is harmful in itself; the problem is excessive or careless use.

**Part 3 Question 1: Should governments regulate children’s use of social media?**

- 普通答案思路：应该，因为孩子自控力弱。
- 7 分答案结构：立场 + 原因 + 具体措施 + 让步。
- 高分表达：
  - Children are not always able to assess online risks.
  - Regulation should focus on platform responsibility, not only parental control.
  - A complete ban may be unrealistic, but age-appropriate safeguards are necessary.

**Part 3 Question 2: Who should be responsible for children’s digital safety: parents, schools or companies?**

- 普通答案思路：都应该负责。
- 7 分答案结构：分层责任 + 企业责任最大 + 家校配合。
- 高分表达：
  - It should be a shared responsibility.
  - Tech companies have the greatest control over design choices.
  - Schools can teach digital literacy, while parents can set daily boundaries.

**Part 3 Question 3: Can technology improve public health?**

- 普通答案思路：可以，比如线上问诊、运动软件。
- 7 分答案结构：具体好处 + 覆盖人群 + 风险。
- 高分表达：
  - Digital tools can make healthcare more accessible.
  - They are especially useful for people in remote areas.
  - However, misinformation can undermine public trust.

**Part 3 Question 4: Why do some cities encourage cycling?**

- 普通答案思路：环保、健康、减少拥堵。
- 7 分答案结构：环境 + 健康 + 城市规划 + 限制条件。
- 高分表达：
  - Cycling can reduce congestion and air pollution.
  - It contributes to a better quality of life.
  - Without safe cycle lanes, people are unlikely to change their habits.
