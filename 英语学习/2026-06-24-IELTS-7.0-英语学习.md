## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”
**中文摘要**
这篇文章讲的是研究人员构建了一种由 AI 驱动的“电脑蠕虫”原型，它能够在设备之间自主传播并学习如何扩大攻击范围。作者指出，这类威胁已经不再只是理论上的假设，而是现实中的网络安全风险。文章强调，现代社会对网络系统依赖极深，从供水、能源、金融到医疗和教育都受到影响，因此 AI 恶意软件的潜在破坏力极大。研究的价值在于，它既暴露了漏洞，也提醒各方必须更认真地准备防御措施。文章整体偏科技新闻风格，观点明确，数据和推断并重，很适合训练你在 IELTS 阅读中快速识别“事实—结论—风险”三层信息。

**核心词汇 / 短语**
1. `autonomously spread` — 自主传播  
   - 原文语境：`a worm ... designed to attack and spread between devices autonomously`
   - 可替换表达：`propagate on its own`

2. `wake-up call` — 警钟，提醒  
   - 原文语境：`the research is a “wake-up call”`
   - 可替换表达：`a strong warning`

3. `woefully underprepared` — 严重准备不足  
   - 原文语境：`the world is woefully underprepared`
   - 可替换表达：`badly unprepared`

4. `self-sustaining` — 可自我维持的  
   - 原文语境：`self-sustaining AI-driven cyber-threats`
   - 可替换表达：`self-perpetuating`

5. `cyber-threat` — 网络威胁  
   - 原文语境：`AI-driven cyber-threats`
   - 可替换表达：`digital threat`

6. `theoretical` — 理论上的  
   - 原文语境：`no longer theoretical`
   - 可替换表达：`hypothetical`

7. `networked computers` — 联网计算机  
   - 原文语境：`modern life dependent on networked computers`
   - 可替换表达：`interconnected systems`

8. `access to` — 获得……的途径 / 对……的使用权  
   - 原文语境：`access to food and goods, energy, our financial system`
   - 可替换表达：`availability of`

**阅读题练习**
1. True / False / Not Given  
   The article says the AI worm has already caused real-world damage across multiple countries.  
   - 答案：`Not Given`
   - 解析：文章说的是研究原型和潜在威胁，没有明确说明它已造成跨国实际破坏。

2. True / False / Not Given  
   The researchers created the worm to help people understand future cyber risks.  
   - 答案：`True`
   - 解析：文章明确提到原型的目的之一是帮助世界准备应对 AI 恶意软件威胁。

3. Multiple Choice  
   Why does the author say the risk is enormous?  
   A. Because the worm is difficult to describe  
   B. Because many essential systems depend on networked computers  
   C. Because AI models are expensive  
   D. Because the study was peer-reviewed  
   - 答案：`B`
   - 解析：文中点明从供水到医疗、教育、金融都依赖联网系统。

4. Matching  
   Match the phrase to the meaning:
   - `wake-up call`
   - `self-sustaining`
   - `woefully underprepared`
   - A. able to continue by itself
   - B. a serious warning
   - C. badly lacking readiness  
   - 答案：`wake-up call → B; self-sustaining → A; woefully underprepared → C`
   - 解析：都是对文章关键风险判断的同义转换。

5. Summary Completion  
   Complete the summary with words from the text:
   The study shows that AI-powered malware is no longer ________. Because modern life depends on ________ computers, the potential impact on systems like health care, education, and finance is extremely ________.  
   - 答案：`theoretical / networked / enormous`
   - 解析：三个空分别对应文章的核心判断、技术基础和风险结论。

**长难句拆解**
句子：`With almost every aspect of modern life dependent on networked computers—drinking water and waste management systems, access to food and goods, energy, our financial system, communications, health care, education, transportation systems, government and so much more—the risk is enormous.`

- 结构：`With + 名词 + 形容词/过去分词短语` 作背景状语，后面主句是 `the risk is enormous`
- 逻辑：前半句先铺垫“现代生活高度依赖联网计算机”，破折号部分是并列举例，后半句推出结论“风险巨大”
- 可模仿表达：`With ... depending on ..., the impact of ... is bound to be ...`

**第二遍精听：听写练习**
请重点听写以下 8 句表达中的关键词与结构：
1. `AI is a 100-year technology`
2. `needs a 100-year response`
3. `the threat of AI-induced economic disruption`
4. `social unrest`
5. `a concrete blueprint`
6. `prepare workers for what’s coming next`
7. `the future of work`
8. `power is matched with responsibility`

**易听错点提醒**
- `AI-induced` 里 `induced` 常被弱读，听成很短的 /ɪnˈdjuːst/
- `100-year` 连读时容易听成一口气的长句
- `responsibility` 和 `possibility` 的尾音要分清
- 数字 `100`、`2026`、`next` 之类容易漏听
- `what’s coming next` 是高频口语表达，注意 `what’s` 的缩略
- `power is matched with responsibility` 里 `matched with` 容易连读

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

You should say:

- why you think it is important
- and explain how society should deal with it

**Part 3 深度追问**
1. Why do some people welcome new technology while others fear it?
2. What kinds of jobs are most vulnerable to automation?
3. Should governments regulate AI more strictly?
4. How can schools prepare students for an AI-driven future?

**每题答案思路 + 7 分结构 + 高分表达**
1. `Do you think AI will change the way people study?`
- 普通思路：会，更方便，能帮我找资料
- 7 分结构：观点 + 例子 + 影响 + 小结
- 高分表达：`It is likely to transform studying by making feedback faster and learning more personalized.`

2. `How often do you use technology in daily life?`
- 普通思路：每天都用，手机电脑都用
- 7 分结构：频率 + 场景 + 依赖程度 + 轻微反思
- 高分表达：`I rely on technology for almost everything, from communication to navigation.`

3. `Do you prefer learning from people or from apps?`
- 普通思路：都可以，老师更好
- 7 分结构：偏好 + 原因 + 对比 + 条件限制
- 高分表达：`Apps are efficient, but human teachers are better at giving nuanced explanations.`

4. `Describe a technology-related issue that may affect society in the future.`
- 普通思路：AI 会抢工作
- 7 分结构：问题定义 + 受影响人群 + 原因 + 对策
- 高分表达：`One major issue is the automation of entry-level jobs, which could widen social inequality unless governments invest in retraining.`

**90 秒口语任务**
请你直接录音或大声回答：
`In 90 seconds, explain one future problem caused by AI and give two practical solutions. Use at least 3 expressions from today’s vocabulary list.`

**自评标准**
- `fluency`：是否能连续说满 90 秒，是否频繁停顿
- `vocabulary`：是否用了 `automation`, `disruption`, `responsibility`, `prepare workers` 等表达
- `grammar`：是否有清晰的从句、因果句、让步句
- `pronunciation`：重音是否自然，连读是否流畅
- `idea development`：是否有观点、原因、例子、结论
