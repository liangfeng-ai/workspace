## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

**文章中文摘要，约 180 词**

文章讨论了美国学校中使用生成式 AI 引发的争议。科技公司和部分教育机构认为，AI 可以帮助学生学习、提升个性化反馈，并让学生提前掌握未来职场需要的 AI literacy。然而，许多家长、儿童发展专家和教师担心，过早让学生依赖 AI 可能削弱他们的批判性思维、写作能力、合作能力和独立解决问题的能力。文章举例说，纽约、俄勒冈等地的家长已经发起请愿或呼吁暂停学生直接使用 AI 工具。一些专家提出“cognitive off-loading”这一概念，即学生把本应由自己完成的思考外包给工具。支持者则强调，AI 对有阅读困难等学习障碍的学生可能有帮助。文章最后呈现了一个核心分歧：学校是否应全面暂停 AI，还是应教学生理解 AI 的局限并批判性使用它。

**8 个核心词汇或短语**

| 词汇/短语 | 中文 | 原文语境 | 可替换表达 |
|---|---|---|---|
| generative AI | 生成式人工智能 | 学生使用聊天机器人完成反馈、写作或学习任务 | AI-powered tools |
| moratorium | 暂停令，暂缓实施 | 家长组织呼吁学校暂停学生使用 AI | temporary ban / suspension |
| cognitive off-loading | 认知外包 | 学生借助 AI 避免自己思考 | outsourcing thinking |
| ubiquitous | 无处不在的 | AI 被描述为未来社会和职场中普遍存在 | widespread / everywhere |
| student-facing tools | 面向学生的工具 | 争议集中在直接给学生使用的 AI 产品 | tools used directly by students |
| foundational academic skills | 基础学术能力 | 家长认为学校应先培养读写、思考等基础能力 | core learning skills |
| guardrails | 保护性规则，限制措施 | 学校需要政策来规范 AI 使用 | safeguards / regulations |
| durable learning | 持久学习 | 专家质疑 AI 是否真正帮助学生形成长期能力 | long-term learning |

**5 道 IELTS 阅读题型练习**

**Questions 1-2: True / False / Not Given**

1. Some parents believe AI tools may encourage children to let machines think for them.  
2. The article states that all American schools have banned student-facing AI tools.

**Questions 3-4: Matching**

Match each view with the correct person or group.

A. Kelly Clancy  
B. Amanda Bickerstaff  
C. Jared Cooney Horvath  
D. New York City council members

3. Students should learn how AI works so that they can use it more critically.  
4. AI may not be suitable for novices because tools designed for experts do not automatically help beginners learn.

**Question 5: Multiple Choice**

5. What is the main purpose of the article?

A. To prove that AI should never be used in schools.  
B. To compare different AI products used by American students.  
C. To show growing concern and debate around AI use in classrooms.  
D. To explain how teachers can use AI to grade homework faster.

**答案与解析**

1. **True**。文章中家长担心 AI 让孩子把思考交给机器，核心是“独立思考被削弱”。  
2. **False**。文章提到一些地方呼吁暂停或限制，但并没有说所有美国学校都已禁止。  
3. **B**。Amanda Bickerstaff 的观点是学生需要了解 AI 的工作方式和限制，从而更批判地使用。  
4. **C**。Horvath 认为专家用来提高效率的工具，不一定适合初学者学习成为专家。  
5. **C**。全文重点不是单方面反 AI，而是呈现家长、专家、公司、学校之间的争议。

**长难句拆解**

句子，改写自文章核心逻辑：

While technology companies argue that AI can personalise learning and prepare students for a future workplace, many parents and child-development experts worry that it may weaken the very thinking skills schools are supposed to build.

- 主干：many parents and experts worry that...
- while 引导让步对比：一边是科技公司的积极观点，一边是家长和专家的担忧。
- that 从句内容：AI may weaken thinking skills。
- the very thinking skills schools are supposed to build：强调“恰恰是学校应该培养的那些思维能力”。
- 可模仿表达：  
  **While some people argue that X can..., others worry that it may...**  
  例句：While online learning can make education more flexible, some teachers worry that it may reduce face-to-face interaction.

## 3. 听力训练：精听 + 抓主旨

**听前预测：5 个可能出现的关键词**

**第一遍泛听任务：回答 3 个主旨问题**

1. What concern does the speaker raise about children interacting with AI-powered toys?  
2. Why does the speaker think children should learn to question AI rather than simply use it?  
3. What kind of solution does the speaker seem to prefer: banning AI, trusting AI, or teaching critical interaction?

**第二遍精听任务：6-10 句关键表达听写练习**

请听第二遍时暂停，写下你听到的对应意思。以下为“训练用改写句”，你要尽量在原演讲中捕捉相近表达：

1. Children are not just using technology; they are forming relationships with it.  
2. AI is increasingly being built into toys, games and everyday objects.  
3. When a machine sounds friendly, children may treat it as if it understands them.  
4. The real question is not whether children will meet AI, but how they will learn to question it.  
5. Kids need opportunities to test, challenge and even break AI systems safely.  
6. Critical thinking should be part of play, not something separate from it.  
7. A toy that gives answers can also shape what a child believes is true.  
8. Adults should help children notice what AI can and cannot do.

**易听错点提醒**

- 连读：`built into` 可能听成 `buildin to`；`not whether` 中 /t/ 弱化。
- 弱读：`can and cannot` 中 `can` 常弱读，`cannot` 更重。
- 近音：`toy` / `tool`，`raise` / `race`，`question` / `suggestion`。
- 转折信号：注意 `but`, `however`, `instead`, `the real question is...`，这些后面常是主观点。
- 抽象名词：`development`, `interaction`, `relationship`, `critical thinking` 是复述时的核心词。
- 人名：Randi Williams，听到名字不用纠结拼写，重点抓身份：AI education researcher。

**听后复述模板，80-120 词**

The talk discusses how children are beginning to interact with AI in more personal ways, especially through toys and digital products. The speaker argues that the issue is not simply whether children should use AI, but whether they can understand and question it. If a machine sounds friendly or intelligent, children may assume that it truly understands them. Therefore, adults should help children explore AI critically. Instead of only banning these tools, schools and parents can create playful activities where children test the limits of AI, notice its mistakes, and learn that technology should be questioned rather than blindly trusted.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

**Part 1 Question 1: Do you often use AI tools for studying?**

- 普通答案思路：说自己偶尔用，用来查词、改句子、整理想法。
- 7 分答案结构：直接回答 + 具体用途 + 限制意识。
- 高分表达：  
  `I use it as a learning assistant rather than a shortcut.`  
  `It helps me organise my ideas, but I still need to do the thinking myself.`

**Part 1 Question 2: Did you use much technology when you were in school?**

- 普通答案思路：以前主要用电脑和手机，现在 AI 更多。
- 7 分答案结构：过去对比现在 + 一个例子 + 简短评价。
- 高分表达：  
  `Technology was more of a supplement back then.`  
  `Now it is becoming embedded in the learning process.`

**Part 1 Question 3: Do you think children should learn how AI works?**

- 普通答案思路：应该，因为他们未来会经常接触 AI。
- 7 分答案结构：观点 + 原因 1 + 原因 2。
- 高分表达：  
  `Basic AI literacy should be treated as part of modern education.`  
  `Children need to understand both the usefulness and the limitations of these tools.`

**Part 2 Cue Card**

Describe a technology that has changed the way people learn.

You should say:

- what the technology is
- how students use it
- what advantages it has
- and explain whether you think it has any risks

- 普通答案思路：讲 AI 学习工具；优点是快、个性化；风险是依赖。
- 7 分答案结构：背景介绍 + 具体使用场景 + 优缺点对比 + 个人判断。
- 高分表达：  
  `It can lower the barrier to learning.`  
  `The downside is that students may confuse convenience with understanding.`  
  `Used wisely, it can support learning; used passively, it can replace effort.`

**Part 3 Question 1: Should schools allow students to use AI in homework?**

- 普通答案思路：可以有限使用，但不能代写。
- 7 分答案结构：分年龄、分任务讨论。
- 高分表达：  
  `A blanket ban may be unrealistic, but unrestricted use is equally problematic.`  
  `Schools should distinguish between using AI for feedback and using it to produce final answers.`

**Part 3 Question 2: What skills will become more important in the age of AI?**

- 普通答案思路：批判思维、沟通、创造力。
- 7 分答案结构：列 2-3 个技能并解释原因。
- 高分表达：  
  `Critical thinking will become more valuable, because information itself is no longer scarce.`  
  `People need to judge the quality of answers, not just generate them quickly.`

**Part 3 Question 3: Could AI replace teachers in the future?**

- 普通答案思路：不能完全替代，老师有情感和引导作用。
- 7 分答案结构：承认 AI 优势 + 强调教师不可替代部分。
- 高分表达：  
  `AI can deliver information, but it cannot fully replace human judgement, empathy and classroom leadership.`  
  `A good teacher does more than answer questions; they notice confusion, motivation and social dynamics.`

**Part 3 Question 4: Who should be responsible for teaching children to use AI safely?**

- 普通答案思路：学校、家长、政府、科技公司都有责任。
- 7 分答案结构：多方责任 + 重点责任。
- 高分表达：  
  `It has to be a shared responsibility.`  
  `Parents can set habits at home, while schools can provide structured digital literacy.`  
  `Tech companies should design products with children’s development in mind.`

**90 秒口语任务**

请录音或大声回答：

Do you think AI helps students become better learners, or does it make them more dependent?

要求：必须包含 1 个具体例子、1 个转折句、1 个个人判断。尽量使用：

- `learning assistant rather than a shortcut`
- `convenience is not the same as understanding`
- `critical thinking`

**自评标准**

- Fluency：是否能连续说 90 秒，中间停顿不超过 3 秒。
- Vocabulary：是否使用了 `AI literacy`, `critical thinking`, `dependency`, `personalised feedback` 等主题词。
- Grammar：是否至少使用 2 个复杂句，如 while / although / because / if。
- Pronunciation：是否清楚区分 `tool`、`toy`、`think`、`thing`。
- Idea development：是否有观点、例子、风险和结论，而不是只说“I think it is good”。
