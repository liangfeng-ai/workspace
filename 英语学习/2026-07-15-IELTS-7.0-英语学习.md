## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章中文摘要

这篇文章介绍了“自主实验室”正在改变科学研究的方式。以加州大学伯克利和劳伦斯伯克利国家实验室的 A-Lab 为例，机器人和 AI 可以在无人值守的情况下连续运行实验，分析结果，并提出下一轮实验方案。这种系统让研究速度大幅提升，例如在材料科学、电池技术和生物技术领域，实验迭代速度可能远超人类研究者。

但文章并没有简单地赞美 AI 科研。它强调，速度并不等于可靠性。A-Lab 早期曾有研究结果受到外部质疑，说明机器虽然能快速生成数据，但科学结论仍需要人类验证、解释和判断。文章还提到，美国政府和科研机构正在把 AI 实验基础设施视为战略重点，因为它可能影响药物研发、能源、水处理和国际科技竞争。最终，作者提出一个核心问题：未来实验室也许可以整夜运行，但科学家仍必须决定这些结果是否可信、是否重要，以及下一步该做什么。

### 答案与解析

1. **True**  
文章明确说明 A-Lab 在夜间无人时仍能运行实验，说明自主实验室可以在没有现场人员的情况下工作。

2. **False**

3. **Not Given**  
文章主题是材料科学、AI 实验、生物技术等，没有说 A-Lab 主要研究语言学习或教育科技。

4. **B**  
文章核心矛盾是：机器能加快科研速度，但数据和结论仍要经过验证，速度不等于可靠性。

5. **B**  
文中提到自主实验室与药物研发、能源、水处理、生物技术竞争等有关，因此政府把它视为战略性科研基础设施。

6. **robots**  
自主实验室的核心组合是 AI、计算资源和机器人。

7. **speed**  
文章反复强调速度带来的机会和问题。

8. **verified**  
机器产生的结果需要由人类验证和解释。

### 长难句拆解

**句子：**  
The episode exposed a central tension in autonomous science: machines can run experiments faster than any human, but the results still have to be verified and interpreted by people.

**结构拆解：**

- 主干：**The episode exposed a central tension**  
  “这一事件暴露了一个核心矛盾。”
- 介词短语：**in autonomous science**  
  限定这个矛盾发生在“自主科学 / 自动化科研”领域。
- 冒号后解释 tension 的具体内容：
  - **machines can run experiments faster than any human**  
    机器做实验比任何人都快。
  - **but the results still have to be verified and interpreted by people**  
    但是结果仍然必须由人类验证和解释。
- 逻辑关系：前半句是优势，后半句是限制，典型 IELTS 阅读中的“让步 + 转折”。

**可模仿表达：**

- AI can process information faster than any individual, but its conclusions still need to be checked by experts.
- Technology can make learning more efficient, but students still need discipline and critical thinking.

### 易听错点提醒

- **连读**：`what we're getting` 容易听成 `what are getting`，注意 /wɪr/ 的弱化。
- **弱读**：`of`, `to`, `and`, `that` 在 TED 演讲中通常很轻，不要逐词硬听。
- **近音词**：`cause` / `course`，`data` / `dater`，`mental` / `metal`。
- **抽象名词**：`culprit`, `evidence`, `generation`, `developmental psychologist` 这类词要结合上下文识别。
- **转折信号**：重点听 `but`, `however`, `instead`, `actually`, `the problem is`，这些后面通常是作者真正观点。
- **列举信号**：当听到多个社会指标时，如 violence, alcohol use, pregnancy，要快速记关键词，不要试图写完整句。

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### IELTS Speaking Part 1

**Question 1: Do you often use technology to study?**

- 普通答案思路：说自己用手机、电脑、App 学英语。
- 7 分答案结构：直接回答 + 具体工具 + 好处 + 小限制。
- 高分表达：
  - I rely on digital tools quite a lot.
  - It helps me learn in a more flexible way.
  - The downside is that I can easily get distracted.

**示范思路：**  
Yes, I rely on technology quite a lot when I study English. For example, I use podcasts to practise listening and online dictionaries to check collocations. It makes learning more flexible because I can study during short breaks. The only downside is that I sometimes get distracted by social media.

---

**Question 2: Do you think young people spend too much time online?**

- 普通答案思路：说是，但也有学习和社交用途。
- 7 分答案结构：承认问题 + 避免绝对化 + 举例 + 总结。
- 高分表达：
  - It depends on how they use it.
  - Passive scrolling can be harmful.
  - Online spaces can also provide support and information.

**示范思路：**  
I think some young people do spend too much time online, especially when they are just scrolling passively. However, I wouldn’t say all screen time is bad. Many students use online platforms to learn, communicate and find support. So the real issue is not just time, but the quality of their online activities.

---

**Question 3: What kind of technology is most useful in your daily life?**

- 普通答案思路：手机最有用。
- 7 分答案结构：选择一个技术 + 解释功能 + 说明影响。
- 高分表达：
  - It has become almost indispensable.
  - It saves me a huge amount of time.
  - I use it for both practical tasks and learning purposes.

**示范思路：**  
My smartphone is probably the most useful technology in my daily life. It has become almost indispensable because I use it for navigation, payment, communication and language learning. It saves me a huge amount of time, but I also need to control how much attention I give to it.

### IELTS Speaking Part 3 深度追问

**Question 1: Do you think technology makes people smarter or lazier?**

- 普通答案思路：两面都有。
- 7 分答案结构：不要二选一；区分“使用方式”；举例；结论。
- 高分表达：
  - It amplifies people’s habits.
  - Used actively, it can expand knowledge.
  - Used passively, it may reduce concentration.

**Question 2: Should schools restrict students’ use of smartphones?**

- 普通答案思路：应该限制，但不能完全禁止。
- 7 分答案结构：承认分心问题 + 提出分场景管理 + 解释原因。
- 高分表达：
  - A blanket ban may be too simplistic.
  - Schools should set clear boundaries.
  - Digital literacy is more realistic than total avoidance.

**Question 3: Why do people often blame technology for social problems?**

- 普通答案思路：因为科技变化快，人们害怕。
- 7 分答案结构：心理原因 + 媒体放大 + 实际问题更复杂。
- 高分表达：
  - Technology is an easy target.
  - Social problems usually have multiple causes.
  - Fear-based narratives spread quickly.

**Question 4: How can society protect young people’s mental health in the digital age?**

- 普通答案思路：家长、学校、平台一起努力。
- 7 分答案结构：多方责任 + 具体措施 + 平衡观点。
- 高分表达：
  - Young people need guidance rather than simple punishment.
  - Platforms should be held accountable for harmful design.
  - Families and schools need to create healthier offline environments.
