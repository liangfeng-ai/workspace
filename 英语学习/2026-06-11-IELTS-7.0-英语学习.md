## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章 150-250 词中文摘要
这篇 Guardian 报道讨论的是：英格兰公立学校教师人数已经连续第二年下降，这和政府此前承诺增加教师招聘形成了明显反差。文章先给出核心事实：学校劳动力普查显示，公立学校教师总数比上一年减少了将近 2000 人。随后文章呈现两种不同说法。教育部强调，按照自己的统计口径，政府已经朝着“在本届议会任期内新增 6500 名教师”的承诺取得了相当进展，尤其是在特殊教育、转介机构和继续教育领域。  
但另一方面，教师工会和教育研究人员指出，这种说法有“统计口径美化”的嫌疑，因为整体教师总量仍在下降，而且新入职教师人数处于至少 15 年来的低位。文章进一步把问题引向更深层：教师短缺不仅影响课堂稳定性，也会加剧 disadvantaged communities 的教育不平等，比如数学课由非专业教师授课的比例仍然偏高。对 IELTS 阅读来说，这篇材料的关键不是死记数字，而是分清“整体人数下降”与“某些领域岗位增加”并不矛盾，同时识别政府说法和第三方评价之间的角度差异。

### 8 个核心词汇或短语
1. `state schools`
   - 中文：公立学校
   - 原文语境：The number of teachers working in England’s state schools has shrunk...
   - 可替换表达：public schools; government-funded schools

2. `school workforce census`
   - 中文：学校劳动力普查
   - 原文语境：The annual school workforce census shows...
   - 可替换表达：school staffing survey; official workforce data

3. `manifesto pledge`
   - 中文：竞选纲领承诺
   - 原文语境：the government was meeting its manifesto pledge...
   - 可替换表达：campaign promise; policy commitment

4. `vacancy rates`
   - 中文：职位空缺率
   - 原文语境：Teacher vacancy rates at record high...
   - 可替换表达：job vacancy levels; unfilled post rates

5. `newly qualified recruits`
   - 中文：新取得资格的招聘教师
   - 原文语境：the number of newly qualified recruits at its lowest...
   - 可替换表达：newly trained teachers; first-time entrants

6. `at a crossroads`
   - 中文：处在十字路口；面临关键选择
   - 原文语境：The government stands at a crossroads on education.
   - 可替换表达：at a turning point; facing a key decision

7. `specialist teaching`
   - 中文：专业教师授课
   - 原文语境：reverse the damage... to the extent of specialist teaching...
   - 可替换表达：subject-specialist instruction; expert subject teaching

8. `disadvantaged communities`
   - 中文：弱势社区
   - 原文语境：schools serving the most disadvantaged communities
   - 可替换表达：underprivileged areas; less advantaged communities

### 答案与解析
1. `False`
   - 解析：文章明确说教师总人数 “drops for second year in row”，并提到比去年少了将近 2000 人。

2. `False`
   - 解析：教育部说的是 “over two-thirds” of the pledge has been met，不是全部完成。

3. `C`
   - 解析：Jack Worth 认为政府把 2024 年 11 月记录到的新增教师也算进来，而那时新政策还不太可能产生实质影响。

4. `B`
   - 解析：这句话来自教师工会负责人 Daniel Kebede，他认为学生人数下降本来可以转化成改善班级规模和机会的窗口。

5. `B`
   - 解析：整篇文章的核心是“整体下滑”与“局部口径下的进展”并存，A、C、D 都过度或偏题。

6. `specialist`
   - 解析：原文是 “non-specialist teachers”，这类题要注意 hyphen 前后的逻辑，不要错写成 `specialists`。

7. `Not Given`
   - 解析：文章只说东部和东南部的比例是 21%，并没有说“下降了”。

### 1 个长难句拆解
原句：
`The DfE argued it had added 4,600 teachers compared with its 2023 baseline, excluding primary teachers.`

拆解：
- 主干：`The DfE argued...`
- 宾语从句：`it had added 4,600 teachers...`
- 比较结构：`compared with its 2023 baseline`
- 补充限制：`excluding primary teachers`

逻辑关系：
这句话的难点不在语法，而在“统计口径”。政府不是说“总教师人数净增 4600”，而是说“和 2023 基线比，在排除小学教师后增加了 4600”。也就是说，它换了一个口径来证明自己在兑现承诺。

可模仿表达：
- `The report showed an improvement compared with last year’s baseline, excluding part-time staff.`
- `The figures look positive, but only under a narrower definition.`

### 第一遍泛听任务：回答 3 个主旨问题
1. Why are colleges described as being “at a breaking point”?
2. What pressures are students facing when they think about higher education?
3. How has the idea of college changed from a public good to a personal investment?

### 第二遍精听任务：选取 8 句关键表达做听写练习
1. America’s colleges have had a rough go of it in recent years.
2. Schools have faced one evolving crisis after another.
3. Students are entering a job market that is beleaguered with uncertainty.
4. AI is already changing how employers are thinking about entry-level jobs.
5. It becomes very difficult to say that you’re coming here to learn how to be a citizen.
6. It’s much harder for us to make the case that the whole investment is worthwhile.
7. There are fewer students to attend school and fewer students to pay for it.
8. College used to be talked about as a public good.

### 易听错点提醒
- `rough go of it` 是整体表达，不要只听成 `rough`
- `one evolving crisis after another` 里 `after another` 常被弱读
- `beleaguered with uncertainty` 词比较书面，容易只抓到 `uncertainty`
- `entry-level jobs` 注意连字符概念，听写时不要漏掉 `level`
- `make the case` 不是“做一个案例”，而是“论证、说明理由”
- `worthwhile` 常被连读成一整块
- `public good` 容易被听成普通的 “good”，但这里是政策讨论中的固定搭配
- 转折信号要特别留意：`but`, `despite`, `not just because`, `and now`

### 听后复述模板：80-120 词英文
The podcast explains that higher education is under pressure from several directions at the same time. Colleges are dealing with financial strain, political interference, demographic decline and the growing impact of AI. Students are also under pressure because tuition is high and the job market feels uncertain, especially for entry-level work. The speakers argue that college is no longer seen mainly as a public good. Instead, many families now treat it as a costly personal investment and expect clear economic returns. This creates a tension between broad education, personal growth and job preparation.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1 Question 1
**Do you think teachers are respected enough in your country?**

- 普通答案思路：
  可以说“口头上尊重，但现实支持不够”，比如工资、压力、家长期待。
- 7 分答案结构：
  先给总体判断 + 说表面尊重和现实待遇的反差 + 举一个学校或社会现象 + 简短总结
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `I would say teachers are respected in principle, but not always supported in practice.`
  - `People expect a lot from teachers, but the working pressure can be overwhelming.`
  - `Social respect does not always translate into better conditions.`

### Part 1 Question 2
**Did you have a favourite teacher when you were at school?**

- 普通答案思路：
  讲一个具体老师，重点不是“他很好”，而是“他怎么影响你”。
- 7 分答案结构：
  点名学科 + 说 teaching style + 说个人影响 + 为什么记得久
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `What stood out was the way he made difficult ideas feel manageable.`
  - `She was demanding, but in a very motivating way.`
  - `That experience changed how I looked at the subject.`

### Part 1 Question 3
**Do you think young people today worry a lot about jobs?**

- 普通答案思路：
  说会，而且比以前更明显，因为学费高、竞争大、AI 不确定。
- 7 分答案结构：
  直接回答 + 两个原因 + 现实后果
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `Yes, I think career anxiety starts much earlier now.`
  - `The path from education to employment feels less predictable than before.`
  - `A lot of students are thinking about employability even before they graduate.`

### Part 2 Cue Card
**Describe an educational problem that you think should be solved.**

You should say:
- what the problem is
- who is affected by it
- why it happens
- and explain how it could be improved

- 普通答案思路：
  可以讲教师短缺、教育资源不均、过度应试、学费压力、AI 带来的学习焦虑。
- 7 分答案结构：
  先定义问题 + 说受影响群体 + 分析成因 + 提出可行改善，不要只抱怨
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `The issue is not just individual; it is structural.`
  - `This problem affects both the quality of learning and long-term social fairness.`
  - `What makes it worse is that the pressure is unevenly distributed.`

### Part 3 深度追问

**1. Why do some countries find it difficult to keep enough teachers in schools?**
- 普通答案思路：
  压力大、薪资吸引力不足、行政事务多、职业回报感下降。
- 7 分答案结构：
  原因 1 + 原因 2 + 长期后果
- 高分表达：
  - `Retention becomes difficult when responsibility rises faster than support.`
  - `Teaching can become less attractive compared with other graduate careers.`

**2. Should governments focus more on teacher recruitment or teacher retention?**
- 普通答案思路：
  两者都要，但 retention 更关键。
- 7 分答案结构：
  先表态 + 解释为什么留住人比不断补人更重要 + 再让步
- 高分表达：
  - `Recruitment matters, but retention is where the system really succeeds or fails.`
  - `There is little point in training new teachers if experienced ones keep leaving.`

**3. Has higher education become too closely linked to employment?**
- 普通答案思路：
  有一点，但也不是完全错，问题在于过度功利化。
- 7 分答案结构：
  承认现实 + 指出风险 + 给平衡观点
- 高分表达：
  - `Higher education has become increasingly market-driven.`
  - `Job preparation is important, but it should not crowd out broader learning.`

**4. How might AI change the value of a university degree?**
- 普通答案思路：
  AI 会改变入门岗位要求，也会逼大学更强调综合能力。
- 7 分答案结构：
  先说变化 + 再说哪些能力更重要 + 最后给审慎结论
- 高分表达：
  - `AI may reduce the value of routine skills while increasing the value of judgment and adaptability.`
  - `A degree alone may matter less than the ability to apply knowledge in complex situations.`

### 90 秒口语任务
请你围绕下面题目连续说 90 秒，并录音或至少大声回答一遍：

**Do you think education systems should focus more on keeping good teachers, or on helping students get jobs faster?**

要求你至少包含：
- 你的总体立场
- 1 个现实层面的原因
- 1 个教育公平层面的原因
- 1 个让步或平衡句

### 自评标准
- `fluency`：是否能连续表达，不频繁卡顿
- `vocabulary`：是否主动用到 `retention`, `public good`, `entry-level jobs`, `disadvantaged communities`, `specialist teaching`
- `grammar`：是否稳定用出让步和因果结构，如 `Although...`, `While...`, `As a result...`
- `pronunciation`：`education`, `retention`, `specialist`, `uncertainty`, `worthwhile` 是否清楚
- `idea development`：是否从“个人看法”扩展到“制度影响”
