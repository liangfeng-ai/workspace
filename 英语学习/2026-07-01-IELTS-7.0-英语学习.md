## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章中文摘要

这篇 Scientific American 文章讨论了一个越来越现实的问题：AI 生成的人脸已经不再像早期那样容易被识别。过去，人们可以通过明显缺陷判断图片是否由 AI 生成，例如皮肤过于光滑、五官不自然、耳朵或眼睛异常等。但随着生成模型进步，这些表面错误越来越少，单靠寻找“视觉漏洞”的方法已经不可靠。

文章介绍了一项新研究：研究人员并不是训练参与者寻找某个模型留下的技术痕迹，而是引导他们观察 AI 人脸的整体特征。AI 生成的人脸往往更接近统计平均值，因此看起来更对称、更比例协调、更“标准化”，但也更缺乏个性、表情和记忆点。相比之下，真实人脸常常包含微小的不对称、独特特征和自然表情，这些“不完美”反而让人更真实、更容易被记住。

文章的核心观点是：识别 AI 假脸的关键不再是寻找单个错误，而是训练自己注意整体模式。这对网络身份验证、社交平台安全和数字时代的信息判断都有现实意义。

| biometric identification | 生物识别身份验证 | 平台用 retinal scans 等方式确认用户是否真实存在 | biological verification / identity authentication |

| technological arms race | 技术军备竞赛 | AI 造假和人类识别能力不断互相升级 | tech competition / escalating battle |

### 雅思阅读题型练习

#### Questions 1-2：True / False / Not Given

#### Questions 3-4：Multiple Choice

   B. These glitches may disappear as AI systems improve.  
   C. Human faces are always symmetrical.  
   D. Biometric scans are cheaper.

4. According to the article, AI-generated faces often appear:  
   A. more expressive and less conventional  
   B. more asymmetrical and memorable  
   C. more balanced but less distinctive  
   D. less attractive but more emotional

#### Question 5：Summary Completion

Complete the summary with ONE WORD from the list below.

AI-generated faces are becoming harder to detect because obvious mistakes are less common. Instead of focusing on temporary visual flaws, researchers suggest looking at broader facial patterns. AI faces often move toward statistical averages: they may look symmetrical and attractive, but they can seem less expressive and less ________ than real human faces.

Word list:  
**memorable / artificial / technical / biometric**

### 答案与解析

1. **True**  
   解析：文章指出早期 AI 人脸常有明显异常，如皮肤、眼睛、耳朵等问题，因此较容易识别。

2. **Not Given**  
   解析：文章提到一些平台使用生物识别来证明真人身份，但没有说这种方法“完美防止欺诈”。

3. **B**  
   解析：只看视觉缺陷的问题是，这些缺陷可能随着软件更新或提示词变化而消失。

4. **C**  
   解析：文章强调 AI 人脸倾向于更对称、更标准、更吸引人，但也更缺乏个性和记忆点。

5. **memorable**  
   解析：文章对比真人与 AI 人脸时指出，AI 人脸通常更不容易被记住。

### 长难句拆解

**句子**：When researchers trained participants to focus not on fleeting visual artifacts but on broader patterns such as symmetry, proportion and expressiveness, their ability to identify AI-generated faces improved significantly.

- **主干**：their ability improved significantly  
- **时间状语从句**：When researchers trained participants...  
- **核心结构**：train someone to focus not on A but on B  
- **A**：fleeting visual artifacts，短暂、容易变化的视觉痕迹  
- **B**：broader patterns such as symmetry, proportion and expressiveness，更整体的模式  
- **逻辑关系**：当训练重点从“局部错误”转向“整体模式”时，识别能力提升。  
- **可模仿表达**：  
  - Instead of focusing on isolated mistakes, learners should pay attention to broader patterns.  
  - The key is not to memorize fixed clues but to understand the underlying logic.

### 易听错点提醒

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Speaking Part 1

#### Question 1：Do you often use social media?

- **普通答案思路**：说自己每天用，用来看新闻、聊天、放松。
- **7 分答案结构**：直接回答 + 使用场景 + 好处 + 小限制。
- **高分表达**：
  - I use it on a daily basis, but I try not to let it dominate my routine.
  - It helps me stay updated and maintain social connections.
  - I set boundaries because it can easily become a time sink.

#### Question 2：Do you think technology makes life easier?

- **普通答案思路**：是的，学习、工作、购物都方便。
- **7 分答案结构**：总体同意 + 举例 + 承认副作用。
- **高分表达**：
  - In many ways, technology has made everyday life far more efficient.
  - That said, convenience sometimes comes at the cost of concentration.
  - It depends on whether we use it deliberately or passively.

#### Question 3：Do you prefer communicating online or face to face?

- **普通答案思路**：熟人面对面，普通消息线上。
- **7 分答案结构**：分场景回答 + 对比两种方式。
- **高分表达**：
  - For quick updates, online communication is more practical.
  - For emotional or serious conversations, face-to-face interaction feels more genuine.
  - Tone and facial expressions are hard to fully capture through a screen.

### Speaking Part 2 Cue Card

#### Describe a time when technology helped you solve a problem.

You should say:

- what the problem was
- what technology you used
- how it helped you
- and explain whether you would use it again

- **普通答案思路**：一次学习/旅行/工作中用手机、AI、地图或翻译软件解决问题。
- **7 分答案结构**：
  1. 简短交代背景  
  2. 描述具体问题  
  3. 说明技术如何一步步帮你  
  4. 总结它的影响和局限  
- **高分表达**：
  - I was under a lot of pressure because I had to deal with it immediately.
  - What made the tool useful was not just speed, but the way it organized information.
  - It did not replace my own judgment, but it gave me a clearer starting point.
  - I would definitely use it again, though I would double-check important details.

### Speaking Part 3 深度追问

#### Question 1：Do you think people rely too much on technology?

- **普通答案思路**：有些人过度依赖，尤其是导航、AI、社交媒体。
- **7 分答案结构**：承认便利 + 指出风险 + 给平衡方案。
- **高分表达**：
  - The issue is not reliance itself, but blind reliance.
  - Technology should enhance human judgment rather than replace it.
  - People need digital literacy, not just digital access.

#### Question 2：Should schools restrict students’ use of smartphones?

- **普通答案思路**：课堂上应该限制，但不能完全禁止。
- **7 分答案结构**：区分课堂纪律和数字能力培养。
- **高分表达**：
  - A complete ban may be too simplistic.
  - Schools should teach students how to use phones responsibly.
  - The goal should be self-regulation rather than pure restriction.

#### Question 3：How can people tell whether online information is reliable?

- **普通答案思路**：看来源、日期、作者，多方核实。
- **7 分答案结构**：来源 + 证据 + 交叉验证 + 警惕情绪化内容。
- **高分表达**：
  - Reliable information usually comes with clear sources and verifiable evidence.
  - If a claim is highly emotional or sensational, I become more cautious.
  - Cross-checking is essential in an age of AI-generated content.

#### Question 4：Will AI make online trust harder in the future?

- **普通答案思路**：会，因为图片、视频、声音都能伪造。
- **7 分答案结构**：先回答会 + 举例 + 说明社会需要新规则。
- **高分表达**：
  - AI will make authenticity harder to judge at first glance.
  - We may need stronger verification systems and better public awareness.
  - Trust will depend less on appearance and more on traceable evidence.
