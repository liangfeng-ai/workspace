## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

**文章中文摘要，约 190 词**

这篇文章讨论了美国学校引入生成式 AI 后引发的担忧。文章以纽约一位家长 Kelly Clancy 的经历开头：她的孩子被要求用 Google Gemini 为科学实验提供反馈，她认为这会让孩子把思考外包给机器。类似担忧正在美国多地扩散，例如俄勒冈州 Bend 有超过 1,100 名家长请愿要求学校移除学生设备上的生成式 AI 工具。文章呈现了两种主要立场：支持者认为 AI 可以帮助学生个性化学习，尤其是帮助有阅读障碍等学习困难的学生；反对者则认为，目前缺乏证据证明 AI 真能促进儿童学习，甚至可能削弱批判性思维和基础能力。文中还提到“cognitive off-loading”，即学生依赖外部工具来避免脑力劳动。最后，文章指出一些教育者并不主张完全禁止 AI，而是希望学生学习 AI 的工作方式和局限；而部分家长则认为学校更应优先培养阅读、写作、解决问题等基础学术能力。

Complete the summary with ONE phrase from the vocabulary list.

**答案与解析**

1. True。她反对的核心是孩子可能让机器替自己思考。
2. False。文章呈现的是多方争议，并非所有教师都反对。

7. C。Horvath 的观点是专家用来提效的工具，不一定适合新手学习。
8. cognitive off-loading; learning disabilities。

**长难句拆解**

句子：While technology companies and policymakers argue that AI can personalise learning and prepare students for a future workplace, many parents and child-development experts remain unconvinced because evidence of its benefits is still limited and the risks to children’s thinking skills are not yet fully understood.

结构拆解：

- 主干：many parents and child-development experts remain unconvinced
- While 引导让步状语从句：technology companies and policymakers argue that...
- argue 后接两个并列宾语从句含义：AI can personalise learning / prepare students for a future workplace
- because 引导原因：evidence is limited / risks are not fully understood
- 逻辑关系：支持者理由让步 + 反对者态度 + 反对原因

可模仿表达：

- While some people argue that..., others remain sceptical because...
- The benefits may be promising, but the evidence remains limited.
- It is not yet clear whether... or merely...

**易听错点提醒**

- 连读：question AI 可能听成 “question-nai”；form emotional bonds 中 form emotional 易连在一起。
- 弱读：should / can / to / of 在 TED 演讲中常弱读，不要每个词都期待重音。
- 近音：toy / tool；bond / bound；raise / race；question / suggestion。
- 抽象名词：development, interaction, curiosity, relationship 容易听懂单词但漏掉逻辑。
- 转折信号：but, however, instead, rather than 后面通常是作者真正立场。
- 人名：Randi Williams，听到名字不必纠结拼写，重点抓身份：AI education researcher。

**听后复述模板，80-120 词**

The talk discusses how children are beginning to interact with AI through toys and other everyday technologies. The speaker argues that the main issue is not simply whether AI is useful or harmful, but whether children understand what these systems can and cannot do. If children form emotional bonds with machines or treat AI as always reliable, their development and judgement may be affected. Therefore, adults should help children question AI in a playful and practical way. Instead of banning technology completely, we can teach children to test it, challenge it and stay curious about how it works.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

**Part 1 Question 1: Do you often use AI tools when studying?**

- 普通答案思路：说会用，用来查资料、改句子、练口语。
- 7 分答案结构：直接回答 + 具体用途 + 限制意识。
- 高分表达：
  - I use it as a study assistant, not as a substitute for thinking.
  - It helps me organise ideas, but I still need to check the accuracy.
  - I try not to rely on it blindly.

**Part 1 Question 2: Did you use much technology at school when you were younger?**

- 普通答案思路：以前主要用课本，现在学生用平板和 AI 更多。
- 7 分答案结构：过去情况 + 与现在对比 + 简短评价。
- 高分表达：
  - Compared with students today, I had a much less digital learning environment.
  - Most of our learning was still textbook-based.
  - Technology was useful, but it was not at the centre of the classroom.

**Part 1 Question 3: Do you think children should learn how AI works?**

- 普通答案思路：应该，因为 AI 会越来越常见。
- 7 分答案结构：明确观点 + 原因 1 + 原因 2。
- 高分表达：
  - Basic AI literacy will probably become as important as digital literacy.
  - Children need to understand both the benefits and the limitations.
  - Knowing how a tool works makes people less likely to be manipulated by it.

**Part 2 Cue Card**

Describe a technology that has changed the way people learn.

You should say:

- what the technology is
- how students use it
- what advantages it has
- and explain whether you think it is mostly positive or negative

**Part 2 普通答案思路**

可以说 AI learning tools。它们能解释概念、改作文、生成练习题，也可能导致依赖和偷懒。结论要平衡：有用，但必须有限制地用。

**Part 2 的 7 分答案结构**

1. 开头点题：I’d like to talk about AI learning tools.
2. 描述用途：students use them for explanations, feedback and language practice.
3. 具体例子：比如用 AI 模拟 IELTS speaking examiner。
4. 优点：即时反馈、个性化、低成本。
5. 风险：过度依赖、错误信息、削弱思考。
6. 总结立场：positive if used critically.

**Part 2 高分表达**

- What makes it powerful is the instant feedback it provides.
- It can lower the barrier to personalised learning.
- The downside is that students may outsource too much of their thinking.
- I’d say it is beneficial only when learners remain in control of the process.

**Part 3 Question 1: Should schools ban students from using AI?**

- 普通答案思路：不应完全禁止，但要限制。
- 7 分答案结构：反对极端做法 + 区分年龄和用途 + 提出规则。
- 高分表达：
  - A blanket ban would be unrealistic.
  - The policy should depend on students’ age and the purpose of use.
  - Schools need clear boundaries rather than vague encouragement.

**Part 3 Question 2: How might AI change the role of teachers?**

- 普通答案思路：老师不会消失，但会从讲知识变成指导学生。
- 7 分答案结构：承认 AI 可处理重复任务 + 强调教师的人类价值。
- 高分表达：
  - Teachers may spend less time delivering basic information and more time guiding students’ thinking.
  - Human judgement, encouragement and classroom management cannot be easily automated.
  - The teacher’s role may shift from knowledge provider to learning coach.

**Part 3 Question 3: What risks might children face if they depend too much on AI?**

- 普通答案思路：思考能力下降、判断力差、社交减少。
- 7 分答案结构：认知风险 + 情感风险 + 学术诚信风险。
- 高分表达：
  - Overdependence could weaken problem-solving habits.
  - Children may mistake machine-generated responses for genuine understanding.
  - It could blur the line between assistance and cheating.

**Part 3 Question 4: Is AI literacy more important than traditional subjects?**

- 普通答案思路：不是替代关系，基础学科仍重要。
- 7 分答案结构：否定二选一 + 说明传统能力是 AI literacy 基础。
- 高分表达：
  - I don’t see it as an either-or choice.
  - Traditional literacy and numeracy are still the foundation.
  - AI literacy should be integrated into subjects rather than taught in isolation.
