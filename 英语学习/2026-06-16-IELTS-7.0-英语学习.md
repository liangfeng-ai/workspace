## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 中文摘要
英国政府宣布将对 16 岁以下用户实施社交媒体禁令，覆盖 Snapchat、TikTok、YouTube、Instagram、X 和 Facebook。文章不是单纯报道政策本身，而是采访多位家长和照护者，呈现出明显分歧：有人觉得这是保护孩子的必要一步，能改变“默认把手机交给孩子”的社会规范；也有人担心这只是修补症状，没有触及算法、平台责任和成瘾机制。部分受访者认为青少年会用 VPN 绕过限制，另一些人则担心脆弱孩子会失去社交支持。文章的核心不是“赞成还是反对”，而是展示一项公共政策在现实中会遇到的执行、责任和公平问题。

### 核心词汇
1. `concrete step` - 具体的一步 - 原文：`a concrete step to protect children` - 可替换：practical measure
2. `the genie is out of the bottle` - 木已成舟 - 原文标题引语 - 可替换：the change is irreversible
3. `accountability` - 责任追究 - 原文：`There must be accountability` - 可替换：responsibility
4. `blanket ban` - 一刀切禁令 - 原文：`A blanket ban on specific apps` - 可替换：general prohibition
5. `bypass` - 绕过 - 原文：`Children will find ways to bypass any ban` - 可替换：get around
6. `predatory companies` - 掠夺性公司 - 原文：`against these predatory companies` - 可替换：exploitative firms
7. `social norm` - 社会规范 - 原文：`the social norm is shifting` - 可替换：public expectation
8. `cut off from` - 切断与...的联系 - 原文：`cut off from avenues of support` - 可替换：deprive of access to

### 题目
1. True/False/Not Given: The article says all parents fully support the under-16 social media ban.
2. True/False/Not Given: Some parents think the ban may push children toward less monitored online spaces.
3. Multiple Choice: What is the main contrast in the article?
   A. Children vs teachers
   B. Support for the ban vs concern about its limits
   C. TikTok vs YouTube
   D. UK vs Australia
4. Matching: Match the view with the speaker type.
   A. Supports the ban as a concrete protective step
   B. Thinks the real problem is the platforms, not the users
   C. Worries vulnerable children may lose support
   1. Parent who says change the algorithms
   2. Parent who welcomes the social norm shift
   3. Parent who says the ban is not a panacea
5. Summary Completion: The article suggests that the ban may change the social `________`, but it may not solve the root problem.

### 答案与解析
1. `False` - 文中明显出现支持与反对两种声音。
2. `True` - 文中直接提到 VPN 和更少监管的网络空间。
3. `B` - 全文重点是“支持 vs 质疑”的对照。
4. 1-B, 2-A, 3-C - 1 对应“change the algorithms”，2 对应欢迎禁令，3 对应 “not a panacea”。
5. `norm` - 原文有 `the social norm is shifting`。

### 长难句拆解
原句：`A blanket ban on specific, named apps will simply drive kids to even less well-monitored networks, potentially increasing the risk of harm.`

拆解：
- 主干：`A blanket ban ... will simply drive kids ...`
- 结果：`potentially increasing the risk of harm`
- 逻辑：先说“禁令”，再说“可能带来反效果”
- 可模仿表达：`A simple fix may solve one problem while creating another.`

### 第一遍泛听任务

2. Who needs to respond to that problem?

### 第二遍精听任务
1. The United States is on track to win the AI race.
2. and hollow itself out in the process
3. AI-induced economic disruption
4. social unrest
5. a concrete blueprint
6. prepare workers for what’s coming next
7. AI is a 100-year technology
8. needs a 100-year response

### 易听错点提醒
- `on track to` 容易漏听成一个词块
- `hollow itself out` 是抽象比喻，别只听成 `hollow`
- `AI-induced` 连读快
- `economic disruption` 重音在 `disruption`
- `concrete blueprint` 不是“蓝图混凝土”

### 听后复述模板
The talk argues that AI will create serious economic and social pressure, especially for workers. The speaker says the U.S. may win the AI race, but still face disruption, instability, and inequality. Her main point is that governments should not treat AI as a short-term trend. Instead, they need long-term policies, worker training, and a broader response that prepares society for structural change.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1
1. Do you think teenagers should have limited access to social media?
2. How do young people in your country usually get news?
3. Do you think technology makes parenting harder?

### Part 2 cue card
Describe a rule that you think is useful for young people.

### Part 3
1. Why do governments try to regulate social media?
2. Do bans usually solve social problems?
3. How should tech companies be held responsible?
4. Is freedom of choice always more important than protection?

### 每题答题框架
- 普通答案思路：先表态，再给一个现实例子
- 7 分答案结构：立场 + 原因 1 + 原因 2 + 让步/平衡
- 可套用表达：
  - `I think the idea makes sense in principle, but the details matter.`
  - `It may reduce one risk, but it does not automatically solve the root cause.`
  - `A better solution would be to...`

### 90 秒口语任务
请围绕这个题目连续说 90 秒，录音或大声回答：

`Do you think governments should ban social media for teenagers? Why or why not?`

### 自评标准
- `fluency`：是否连续、少停顿
- `vocabulary`：是否用了 `ban`, `responsibility`, `algorithm`, `support`, `harm`
- `grammar`：是否有 `although`, `if`, `while`, `not only... but also...`
- `pronunciation`：`algorithm`, `responsibility`, `social`, `government`
- `idea development`：是否讲到了“孩子、家长、平台、政府”四层
