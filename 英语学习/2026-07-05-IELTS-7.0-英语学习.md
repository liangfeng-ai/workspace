## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章中文摘要

这篇文章讨论了美国学校中生成式 AI 工具快速进入课堂后引发的担忧。支持者认为，AI 可以帮助教师提高效率，也能为有阅读障碍等学习困难的学生提供辅助，例如把文字转为语音或提供个性化反馈。然而，许多家长、儿童发展专家和教育研究者认为，目前几乎没有充分证据证明生成式 AI 能真正促进儿童学习，反而可能让学生把思考过程外包给机器，形成“认知卸载”。文章提到，一些家长组织要求学校暂停学生直接使用 AI 工具，纽约市部分议员也呼吁设置两年暂停期。专家指出，成年人或专业人士用 AI 提高效率，和儿童用 AI 学习基础能力并不是一回事。文章最后呈现了两种立场：一方主张限制或暂停学生使用生成式 AI，优先保护基础学术能力；另一方认为完全禁止并不现实，学生需要学习 AI 的局限并培养批判性使用能力。

### 8 个核心词汇或短语

| 英文 | 中文 | 原文语境 | 可替换表达 |
|---|---|---|---|
| prompt a tide of concern | 引发大量担忧 | AI entering classrooms has triggered broad concern among parents and experts. | spark widespread worry / raise alarm |
| student-facing AI tools | 面向学生使用的 AI 工具 | Some groups want schools to remove AI tools used directly by children. | AI tools used by students / learner-facing tools |
| cognitive off-loading | 认知卸载 | Experts worry students may avoid mental effort by relying on AI. | outsourcing thinking / mental shortcut |

### 雅思阅读题型练习

#### Questions 3-4：Multiple Choice

#### Question 5：Summary Completion

Complete the summary with words from the box.

Some parents and experts argue that schools should not rush to introduce generative AI because there is still ________ that it improves children’s learning. They worry that students may rely on AI instead of building ________.

Words:  
A. little evidence  
B. foundational academic skills  
C. entertainment habits  
D. teacher salaries

### 答案与解析

1. **True**。文章中家长担心 AI 会让孩子把思考交给机器，核心是“机器代替思考”。  
2. **False**。文章提到一些家长组织、议员和学校系统呼吁限制或暂停，但没有说全美所有学校已经禁止。  
3. **B**。专家担心的是 cognitive off-loading，即学生减少主动思考和深度加工。  
4. **C**。支持者提到 AI 可帮助有阅读障碍等学习困难的学生。  
5. **A / B**。完整句意：证据不足；学生应建立基础学术能力。

### 长难句拆解

**句子**：  
While big tech and government leaders have pushed teachers to use AI and claimed it helps students learn, some parents and child development experts argue that there is little evidence that AI helps children and may even harm their cognitive development.

**结构拆解**：

- **While...**：让步状语从句，表示“尽管……”
- **big tech and government leaders have pushed... and claimed...**：两个并列动作，说明支持方的立场
- **some parents and experts argue that...**：主句，提出反方观点
- **there is little evidence that...**：同位语式内容，说明“证据不足”
- **may even harm...**：递进逻辑，表示不仅可能无益，还可能有害

**可模仿表达**：

- While some people argue that technology improves efficiency, others worry that it may weaken essential human skills.
- Although AI can offer instant support, there is limited evidence that it leads to deeper learning.

### 易听错点提醒

- **连读**：`think of it` 容易听成 `thinkovit`；`part of the brain` 容易连成 `partəv the brain`。  
- **弱读**：`to / of / and / that` 在 BBC 对话里常弱读，不要逐词硬抓。  
- **近音**：`natural` 和 `neural` 容易混；本集主要是 `natural`。  
- **专业词**：`dedicated`, `co-opt`, `circuit`, `intricate` 要提前熟悉发音。  
- **转折信号**：重点听 `but`, `however`, `so`, `because`，它们后面常出现答案。  
- **人名**：Maryanne Wolf、Rebecca Gotlieb 不必拼写完美，但要听出后面观点。

### 听后复述模板：80-120 词英文

Today’s episode explains that reading is not a natural ability in the same way as speaking. Human brains do not have a special area designed only for reading, so when we learn to read, we use and connect different parts of the brain, such as areas related to vision, sound, language and attention. The programme also points out that different writing systems may shape the brain in different ways. For example, reading Chinese characters may require more visual memory and processing than reading alphabet-based languages. Overall, the episode suggests that deep reading is not just a school skill, but an activity that changes how we think.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1 问题 1

**Question**：Do you often use AI tools when you study?

- **普通答案思路**：说会用，比如查单词、改句子、找资料；但不会完全依赖。
- **7 分答案结构**：直接回答 + 具体用途 + 风险意识 + 个人边界。
- **高分表达**：
  - I use AI as a learning assistant rather than a replacement for thinking.
  - It is helpful for quick feedback, but I still need to process the information myself.
  - I try not to outsource the difficult part of learning.

### Part 1 问题 2

**Question**：Do you prefer reading on paper or on a screen?

- **普通答案思路**：纸质书更专注，屏幕更方便。
- **7 分答案结构**：偏好 + 场景区分 + 原因 + 小例子。
- **高分表达**：
  - For deep reading, I definitely prefer paper.
  - Screens are convenient, but they also make me more likely to skim.
  - Paper helps me slow down and engage with the text more carefully.

### Part 1 问题 3

**Question**：What kind of technology is useful in education?

- **普通答案思路**：词典、网课、录音、AI 反馈等。
- **7 分答案结构**：分类回答 + 举例 + 限制条件。
- **高分表达**：
  - Technology is useful when it supports active learning.
  - It should enhance classroom interaction, not replace it.
  - The key is whether students are still doing the mental work.

### Part 2 Cue Card

**Describe a time when technology helped you learn something.**

You should say:

- what technology you used
- what you were trying to learn
- how it helped you
- and explain whether you think this technology changed the way you learn

**普通答案思路**：讲用 AI / app / YouTube 学英语，帮助查词、纠音、精听。  
**7 分答案结构**：

1. 背景：我在准备雅思，听力和口语较弱。  
2. 工具：使用 BBC 音频、AI 纠错、录音软件。  
3. 过程：先听主旨，再精听，再复述。  
4. 反思：技术提高效率，但真正进步来自主动输出。

**高分表达**：

- It made the learning process more interactive and less frustrating.
- I could get immediate feedback instead of waiting for a teacher.
- However, I realised that convenience does not automatically lead to improvement.
- The biggest change was that I became more aware of my own mistakes.

### Part 3 深度追问 1

**Question**：Should schools allow young children to use generative AI?

- **普通答案思路**：不应该太早用，容易依赖；可以在老师指导下了解。
- **7 分答案结构**：立场 + 年龄区分 + 风险 + 有条件使用。
- **高分表达**：
  - I would be cautious about giving young children direct access to generative AI.
  - At that age, they are still developing basic literacy and reasoning skills.
  - AI should be introduced as something to question, not something to blindly follow.

### Part 3 深度追问 2

**Question**：Can AI replace teachers in the future?

- **普通答案思路**：不能完全替代，老师有情感支持和判断力。
- **7 分答案结构**：承认 AI 优势 + 强调教师不可替代 + 例子。
- **高分表达**：
  - AI may automate certain tasks, but it cannot replace the human side of teaching.
  - A good teacher does more than deliver information.
  - Teachers can notice confusion, encourage students and adapt to classroom dynamics.

### Part 3 深度追问 3

**Question**：Why is deep reading important in modern society?

- **普通答案思路**：帮助思考、理解复杂问题、避免碎片化。
- **7 分答案结构**：定义 deep reading + 现代问题 + 价值。
- **高分表达**：
  - Deep reading trains patience, concentration and critical thinking.
  - In an age of short videos and instant answers, this skill is becoming rarer.
  - It allows people to evaluate arguments instead of just consuming information.

### Part 3 深度追问 4

**Question**：What responsibilities do governments have when introducing AI into education?

- **普通答案思路**：制定规则、保护隐私、评估效果。
- **7 分答案结构**：三点展开：证据、隐私、公平。
- **高分表达**：
  - Governments should require evidence before large-scale adoption.
  - They need to protect children’s data and mental development.
  - Public education should not become a testing ground for private technology companies.
