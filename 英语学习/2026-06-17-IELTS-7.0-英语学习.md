## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章 150-250 词中文摘要
这篇报道引用联合国儿童基金会的数据指出，全球约一半儿童正在同时暴露于至少三种气候风险之中，例如热浪、洪水、干旱、空气污染和水资源短缺。文章强调，这已经不只是低收入国家的问题，几乎所有国家的儿童都至少面临一种气候威胁，高收入国家也不例外。报道通过具体案例说明，气候风险会直接影响儿童上学、饮水、健康和营养状况，例如一些地区的孩子必须冒险穿越河流去上学，另一些地区则长期承受高温和缺水。文章的核心意思不是单纯渲染灾难，而是指出儿童受到的伤害具有叠加效应：环境风险、贫困、基础设施不足和公共卫生压力会同时出现。对 IELTS 阅读来说，这篇文章很适合训练你识别“数据 + 例子 + 结论”的新闻结构，并快速抓住文章真正的中心句。

### 8 个核心词汇或短语
1. `climate hazards`
   - 中文：气候风险 / 气候灾害因素
   - 原文语境：`children exposed to at least three climate hazards`
   - 可替换表达：climate threats

2. `exposed to`
   - 中文：暴露于
   - 原文语境：`children exposed to at least three climate hazards`
   - 可替换表达：subject to

3. `high-income countries`
   - 中文：高收入国家
   - 原文语境：`including those from high-income countries`
   - 可替换表达：wealthier nations

4. `heatwaves`
   - 中文：热浪
   - 原文语境：`Millions of children in Italy are exposed to prolonged heatwaves`
   - 可替换表达：periods of extreme heat

5. `drought`
   - 中文：干旱
   - 原文语境：`prolonged heatwaves or drought`
   - 可替换表达：water shortage crisis

6. `prolonged`
   - 中文：长期的，持续的
   - 原文语境：`prolonged heatwaves`
   - 可替换表达：extended

7. `public health`
   - 中文：公共健康
   - 原文语境：文章将气候暴露与儿童健康风险并列讨论
   - 可替换表达：community health

8. `compound risk`
   - 中文：复合风险，叠加风险
   - 原文语境：文章主旨体现为多个风险同时作用于儿童
   - 可替换表达：combined threat

### 5 道雅思阅读题型练习

#### 题目 1-2：True / False / Not Given
1. The article says climate hazards mainly affect children in low-income countries.
2. The report suggests that children can face more than one climate-related danger at the same time.

#### 题目 3：Multiple Choice
3. What is the main purpose of the article?
   A. To argue that climate risk is exaggerated in the media  
   B. To show that children around the world face overlapping climate dangers  
   C. To compare European and African school systems  
   D. To explain how UNICEF was founded

#### 题目 4：Matching
4. Match each example with the climate hazard mentioned in the article.
   A. Children in Italy
   B. Children crossing a river to get to school
   C. Children mentioned in the UNICEF global warning

   1. Exposure to multiple hazards
   2. Flood or dangerous water access
   3. Heatwaves or drought

#### 题目 5：Summary Completion
5. Complete the summary with ONE word only.  
   The article argues that climate threats to children are not isolated. Instead, they often come in `________` and affect schooling, health and safety at the same time.

### 答案与解析
1. `False`
   - 解析：文章明确说几乎所有儿童都至少面临一种气候风险，连高收入国家的儿童也包括在内，因此不是“主要只影响低收入国家”。

2. `True`
   - 解析：标题和正文核心信息就是“至少三种气候风险”，说明多种风险会同时存在。

3. `B`
   - 解析：全文重点不是介绍 UNICEF，也不是比较国家，而是强调儿童面临的是 overlapping / multiple climate dangers。

4. `A-3, B-2, C-1`
   - 解析：Italy 对应 prolonged heatwaves or drought；过河上学对应危险水域；UNICEF 的全球结论对应 multiple hazards。

5. `combination`
   - 解析：这里考查你是否理解文章中心逻辑，即风险不是单独出现，而是成组、叠加地出现。若按原意复述，`combination` 最合适。

### 长难句拆解
原句：`Almost every child, including those from high-income countries, is now exposed to at least one hazard.`

- 主干：`Almost every child is now exposed to at least one hazard.`
- 插入成分：`including those from high-income countries`
- 逻辑关系：主句先给出全球性判断，插入成分再强调“连高收入国家也不例外”，起到补充和强化作用。
- 你要学会的点：
  - `including ...` 常用于补充举例或强化范围。
  - `be exposed to` 是 IELTS 高频表达，可直接迁移到环境、健康、教育不平等等话题。
- 可模仿表达：
  - `Many teenagers, including those in large cities, are exposed to constant online pressure.`

## 3. 听力训练：精听 + 抓主旨

### 听前预测：根据标题预测 5 个可能出现的关键词
1. extreme weather
2. drought
3. early warning
4. farmers
5. food security

### 第一遍泛听任务：回答 3 个主旨问题
1. What gap does the speaker identify between technology and real-life action?
2. Why does the speaker think early warning systems are still failing many communities?
3. What kind of response does she argue for besides better data?

### 第二遍精听任务：6-10 句关键表达听写
1. Thanks to advanced technology, we can now see droughts and crop failures months before they hit.
2. So why are millions of people still going hungry?
3. The problem is not only prediction, but what happens after prediction.
4. Early warning does not automatically become early action.
5. Communities need information they can actually use.
6. Data alone cannot deliver seeds, credit or irrigation.
7. The messy middle between science and action is where many systems fail.
8. We should measure success by improved lives, not just smarter models.

### 易听错点提醒
- `crop failures` 中 `p` 和 `f` 连起来很快，容易只听到 `failures`
- `early warning` 和 `early action` 很容易混淆，要特别听后半部分名词
- `data alone cannot...` 里的 `alone` 弱读后容易漏掉，导致意思反了
- `credit or irrigation` 属于信息密度高的并列结构，第二遍要专门卡词
- `messy middle` 是比喻表达，第一次可能听得出来单词，未必能立刻理解意思
- `improved lives` 和 `smarter models` 是演讲结尾式对比，要注意重音落点

### 听后复述模板：80-120 词英文
The speaker argues that modern technology can already predict droughts and crop failures well in advance, but many vulnerable communities still do not benefit from that knowledge. She says the real problem lies in the gap between scientific prediction and practical action. Data by itself cannot provide farmers with seeds, loans or irrigation. To improve climate resilience, governments and organizations need to connect information with local support systems. Her main message is that success should be measured not only by accurate models, but by whether people’s lives actually become safer and more stable.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1 问题 1
**Question:** Do you often pay attention to weather reports?

- 普通答案思路：说你会不会看天气预报，通常是为了出行、穿衣或安排工作。
- 7 分答案结构：直接回答 + 个人习惯 + 一个具体场景 + 小延伸
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `I check weather updates almost every day, mainly for practical reasons.`
  - `It helps me plan ahead instead of making last-minute decisions.`

### Part 1 问题 2
**Question:** Has the weather in your city changed in recent years?

- 普通答案思路：说更热、更不稳定、下雨更突然，联系日常体验。
- 7 分答案结构：总体变化 + 一个具体表现 + 对生活的影响
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `The weather has become noticeably more extreme.`
  - `What used to feel seasonal now feels unpredictable.`

### Part 1 问题 3
**Question:** Do you think children should learn more about climate issues at school?

- 普通答案思路：支持，理由是这和生活、健康、安全都有关。
- 7 分答案结构：立场 + 为什么必要 + 学校怎么教更有效
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `I think climate education should be practical rather than purely theoretical.`
  - `Children need to understand how global issues affect daily life.`

### Part 2 Cue Card
**Describe a time when you learned about a serious environmental problem.**  
You should say:
- when it was
- where you got the information
- what the problem was
- and explain why it impressed you

- 普通答案思路：可以讲看新闻、短视频、学校课程或一次极端天气事件。
- 7 分答案结构：
  - 背景：什么时候、通过什么渠道知道
  - 内容：是什么问题
  - 影响：为什么让你印象深刻
  - 反思：它改变了你对社会或个人责任的看法
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `What struck me most was that the issue felt distant at first, but it was actually closely connected to everyday life.`
  - `It made me realize that environmental problems are not abstract scientific topics; they are social and human problems as well.`

### Part 3 深度追问 1
**Question:** Why are children often more vulnerable to climate-related problems than adults?

- 普通答案思路：身体更脆弱、依赖成人、资源更少。
- 7 分答案结构：生理原因 + 社会原因 + 长期后果
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `Children are more vulnerable both physically and socially.`
  - `They usually have less control over their environment and fewer resources to protect themselves.`

### Part 3 深度追问 2
**Question:** What should governments prioritise when dealing with extreme weather?

- 普通答案思路：预警系统、基础设施、公共服务。
- 7 分答案结构：先说 priority，再说 why，再补一个现实障碍
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `In my view, early warning systems are important, but they are not enough on their own.`
  - `The real priority is turning information into fast and practical support.`

### Part 3 深度追问 3
**Question:** Do you think technology can solve most climate problems?

- 普通答案思路：不能单靠技术，还需要政策和执行。
- 7 分答案结构：部分同意 + 限制 + 补充条件
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `Technology is a tool, not a complete solution.`
  - `Without policy coordination and public trust, even advanced tools can have limited impact.`

### Part 3 深度追问 4
**Question:** How can the media influence public understanding of environmental risk?

- 普通答案思路：媒体既能提高意识，也可能制造恐慌或简化问题。
- 7 分答案结构：正面作用 + 负面风险 + 理想做法
- 可直接套用的高分表达：
  - `The media can make complex issues more visible to the general public.`
  - `At the same time, oversimplified coverage may create emotion without real understanding.`

### 90 秒口语任务
请你围绕这个题目连续说 90 秒：  
`Do governments need to do more than just warn people about climate risks?`

要求：
- 先明确表态
- 至少给出 2 个理由
- 至少举 1 个现实例子
- 结尾给出一句总结判断

### 自评标准
- `fluency`：有没有明显卡顿，能否持续表达 90 秒
- `vocabulary`：是否用了 climate risk, early warning, vulnerable groups, practical support 这类主题词
- `grammar`：是否能稳定使用复杂句，而不只是短句堆叠
- `pronunciation`：关键词是否清晰，尤其是 climate, vulnerable, infrastructure, prediction
- `idea development`：观点有没有展开，而不是只重复同一句意思
