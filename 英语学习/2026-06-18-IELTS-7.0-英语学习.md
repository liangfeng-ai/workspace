## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章中文摘要

这篇阅读材料讨论了人工智能正在如何改变学校教育。文章通常会以某所学校或课堂为例，展示 AI 工具如何帮助教师分析学生的学习表现、提供个性化练习、即时反馈作业，并减少部分重复性工作。支持者认为，AI 可以让学生按照自己的节奏学习，帮助教师更快发现学生的薄弱点，也可能改善教育资源不均的问题。然而，文章也会指出一些担忧，例如学生过度依赖 AI、教师角色被削弱、数据隐私问题，以及 AI 给出的答案可能存在偏见或错误。更重要的是，教育专家通常强调 AI 不应取代教师，而应作为辅助工具。真正有效的教育仍然需要人类教师提供情感支持、价值判断、课堂互动和批判性思维训练。整体来看，文章不是简单赞美或否定 AI，而是呈现一种平衡观点：AI 有潜力提升学习效率，但前提是学校、教师和学生能够合理、透明并负责任地使用它。

AI may help students by giving faster 6. ______ and supporting different learning needs. However, experts warn that schools must consider issues such as data 7. ______ and possible algorithmic 8. ______.

### 答案与解析

1. **True**  
解析：文章核心观点之一是 AI 可以通过数据分析帮助教师更快发现学生问题，属于同义替换：identify weaknesses = detect learning gaps。

2. **False**  
解析：文章强调 AI 是辅助工具，而不是完全替代教师。completely replace 是绝对化表达，通常与原文相反。

3. **Not Given**  
解析：即使文章提到某些学校使用 AI，也不等于所有英国学校都全面使用。题干中的 all 和 every classroom 信息过度扩大。

4. **B**  
解析：个性化学习的核心优势就是让学生按照更适合自己的节奏学习。A、C、D 都过于绝对。

5. **C**  
解析：文章既讲潜力，也讲风险，因此态度是 balanced and cautious。

6. **feedback**  
解析：faster feedback = real-time feedback / immediate response。

7. **privacy**  
解析：data privacy 是 AI 教育话题中的高频风险点。

8. **bias**  
解析：algorithmic bias 指算法偏见，是人工智能伦理讨论中的核心表达。

### 长难句拆解

**句子**：  
While AI-powered tools can provide students with personalised feedback and help teachers identify learning gaps more efficiently, experts warn that they should be used to support rather than replace human interaction in the classroom.

**结构拆解**：  
- **While...**：让步状语从句，表示“尽管……”  
- **AI-powered tools can provide... and help...**：并列谓语，说明 AI 的两个好处  
- **experts warn that...**：主句，表达专家提醒  
- **should be used to support rather than replace...**：核心观点，表示“应该用来支持，而不是取代……”  

**逻辑关系**：  
先承认 AI 的优势，再提出限制和警示，是 IELTS 阅读和写作中非常常见的平衡论证结构。

**可模仿表达**：  
While technology can improve efficiency and access to information, it should be used to enhance rather than replace human judgment.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1 Question 1

**Question**：Do you often use technology when you study?

**普通答案思路**：  
说自己经常用手机、电脑、词典、网课，帮助查资料和练听力。

**7 分答案结构**：  
直接回答 + 具体工具 + 具体用途 + 小评价

**高分表达**：  
- I rely on digital tools quite a lot when studying.  
- They help me study more efficiently.  
- I use them mainly for looking up unfamiliar words and practising listening.

**示范答案**：  
Yes, I rely on digital tools quite a lot when studying. For example, I use online dictionaries to check pronunciation and collocations, and I often watch English videos to improve my listening. I think technology makes learning more flexible, although it can also be distracting if I don’t manage my time well.

### Part 1 Question 2

**Question**：Do you prefer learning online or in a classroom?

**普通答案思路**：  
线上方便，课堂互动更好，可以说两者结合最好。

**7 分答案结构**：  
偏好 + 对比 + 原因 + 补充条件

**高分表达**：  
- Online learning gives me more flexibility.  
- Face-to-face classes are better for interaction.  
- A blended approach works best for me.

**示范答案**：  
I would say a blended approach works best for me. Online learning gives me more flexibility because I can review lessons whenever I want. However, face-to-face classes are better for discussion and immediate feedback. So I prefer using online resources as a supplement rather than a complete replacement.

### Part 1 Question 3

**Question**：What kind of apps do you use for learning English?

**普通答案思路**：  
词典、听力软件、视频平台、AI 工具。

**7 分答案结构**：  
列举 2-3 类 + 各自用途 + 效果

**高分表达**：  
- pronunciation practice  
- vocabulary review  
- exposure to authentic English  
- track my progress

**示范答案**：  
I mainly use dictionary apps, podcast apps and video platforms. Dictionary apps help me check pronunciation and example sentences, while podcasts give me exposure to authentic English. I also use some vocabulary apps to review words regularly and track my progress.

### Part 2 Cue Card

**Describe a piece of technology that helps you study.**

You should say:  
- what it is  
- how you use it  
- how often you use it  
- and explain why it is useful for your study

**普通答案思路**：  
可以讲 AI 工具、词典 App、YouTube、播客 App。重点说它如何提升效率、纠错、提供材料。

**7 分答案结构**：  
背景介绍 → 使用方式 → 具体例子 → 优点与限制 → 总结评价

**高分表达**：  
- It has become an essential part of my study routine.  
- It saves me a considerable amount of time.  
- It gives me instant feedback.  
- I try not to rely on it blindly.  
- It works best when I use it as a learning assistant.

**示范答案框架**：  
One piece of technology that really helps me study is an online dictionary app. I use it almost every day, especially when I read English articles or prepare for IELTS speaking. What I find most useful is that it does not only give me the meaning of a word, but also shows pronunciation, example sentences and common collocations. For example, if I look up the word “efficient”, I can also learn phrases like “an efficient way” or “improve efficiency”. This helps me use vocabulary more naturally. Another advantage is that it saves me a lot of time, because I can check words instantly. However, I try not to rely on it blindly. I usually write down useful expressions and make my own sentences afterwards. Overall, I think it is a practical learning assistant rather than just a translation tool.

### Part 3 Question 1

**Question**：How has technology changed the way students learn?

**普通答案思路**：  
学习更方便、资源更多、反馈更快，但注意力更容易分散。

**7 分答案结构**：  
总体变化 + 两个积极影响 + 一个问题 + 总结

**高分表达**：  
- Technology has made learning more accessible and personalised.  
- Students are no longer limited to textbooks.  
- The downside is that it may reduce concentration.  
- It depends on how students use it.

### Part 3 Question 2

**Question**：Do you think AI can replace teachers in the future?

**普通答案思路**：  
不能完全替代。AI 可以辅助，但老师有人情味、能引导价值观和互动。

**7 分答案结构**：  
明确立场 + AI 优势 + 教师不可替代点 + 平衡总结

**高分表达**：  
- I don’t think AI can fully replace teachers.  
- It can handle repetitive tasks and provide instant feedback.  
- Teaching is not just about delivering information.  
- Emotional support and moral guidance still require human teachers.

### Part 3 Question 3

**Question**：What are the risks of using too much technology in education?

**普通答案思路**：  
依赖、注意力下降、隐私风险、批判性思维变弱。

**7 分答案结构**：  
列出风险 + 举例 + 解释后果 + 解决方法

**高分表达**：  
- Students may become overly dependent on digital tools.  
- Their ability to think independently might be weakened.  
- Data privacy is another serious concern.  
- Schools should set clear rules for responsible use.

### Part 3 Question 4

**Question**：Should schools invest more money in educational technology?

**普通答案思路**：  
应该，但不能只买设备，也要培训老师、保护隐私、关注公平。

**7 分答案结构**：  
有条件同意 + 投资好处 + 必要配套 + 总结

**高分表达**：  
- I agree, but only if the investment is carefully planned.  
- Buying devices is not enough.  
- Teachers need proper training.  
- Technology should narrow the education gap rather than widen it.
