## 2. 阅读训练：从“看懂”到“雅思阅读速度”

### 文章中文摘要 150-250 词
这篇文章讨论 AI 驱动的大规模监控可能对社会进步造成的长期影响。作者认为，未来的监控系统不再只是记录某些违法行为，而是可能通过人脸识别、数字追踪、社交媒体监控、数据库整合和自动化执法，持续观察人们在公共和私人空间中的行为。表面上，这类技术常被包装为维护安全、提高效率或减少犯罪的工具，但作者强调，它真正危险的地方在于“寒蝉效应”：当人们知道自己随时可能被识别、记录、评价或惩罚时，就会减少表达、抗议、试验新生活方式或挑战主流观念的意愿。文章还指出，历史上的社会进步往往依赖少数群体、反主流文化和公共讨论空间；如果 AI 监控让人们更顺从、更自我审查，民主社会中的创造力、异议和改革动力都会被削弱。最后，作者提出可以通过限制人脸识别、加强隐私保护、监管 AI 使用和约束政府与科技公司的权力来避免这种未来。

### 5 道 IELTS 阅读题型练习

#### Questions 1-3：True / False / Not Given
判断下列说法是否与文章观点一致。

1. The authors believe AI surveillance may affect not only personal freedom but also democratic development.  
2. The article argues that AI surveillance has already eliminated most public protests in Western countries.  
3. The authors suggest that banning or restricting facial recognition could reduce the risks of mass surveillance.

#### Questions 4-5：Multiple Choice
4. What is the main concern of the article?  
A. AI systems are too expensive for governments to maintain.  
B. AI surveillance may make people more afraid to express or experiment.  
C. Facial recognition is inaccurate in all situations.  
D. AI will completely replace human police officers.

5. According to the article, why are historical examples of surveillance not fully comparable to modern AI surveillance?  
A. Past systems were less technologically integrated and automated.  
B. Past systems were always more democratic.  
C. Modern systems collect less personal data.  
D. Modern surveillance is only used in science fiction.

### 答案与解析
1. **True**。文章核心观点是 AI 监控不仅影响个人自由，还会压制民主社会所需的异议、创造力和社会进步。  
2. **Not Given**。文章提到监控可能影响抗议者、记者和异议者，但没有说西方国家的公共抗议已经“大多消失”。  
3. **True**。作者明确提出限制人脸识别、加强隐私保护和监管 AI 是可能的政策路径。  
4. **B**。文章重点不是成本或技术准确率，而是“人们因为害怕被监控而自我审查”。  
5. **A**。作者认为现代 AI 监控的特点是数据融合、自动化分析、实时识别和大规模部署，远超传统监控。

### 长难句拆解
**句子**：If AI surveillance is deployed at scale, it may not simply punish illegal behaviour, but gradually reshape what people dare to say, do and imagine in public life.

- **主干**：it may reshape what people dare to say, do and imagine  
- **条件状语**：If AI surveillance is deployed at scale  
- **not simply...but...**：不是简单地……而是……，用于强调更深层后果  
- **what 引导宾语从句**：what people dare to say, do and imagine，表示“人们敢说、敢做、敢想的事情”  
- **可模仿表达**：  
  - If technology is used without safeguards, it may not simply improve efficiency, but fundamentally change human behaviour.  
  - If schools rely too heavily on AI, they may not simply save time, but reshape how students think and learn.

### 第二遍精听任务：6-10 句关键表达听写练习
说明：以下为**基于 TED 内容的改写版听写句**，用于训练学术听力表达和 IELTS Section 4 记录能力。听完原材料后，尝试根据意思补全或复写。

1. The human cell is far more complex than any machine we have ever built.  
2. Many diseases remain difficult to treat because we do not fully understand what goes wrong inside cells.  
3. AI may help scientists detect patterns that would be impossible for humans to see alone.  
4. A virtual cell could allow researchers to test ideas before moving into the laboratory.  
5. The goal is not to replace biology, but to make biological research faster and more precise.  
6. If this approach works, it could change how new drugs are discovered.  
7. Researchers need enormous amounts of reliable data to train such a model.  
8. The promise of AI in medicine depends on both scientific ambition and careful validation.

### 易听错点提醒
- **连读**：`what goes wrong inside cells` 中 `goes wrong` 容易连在一起。  
- **弱读**：`could allow researchers to` 中 `could` 和 `to` 会弱读。  
- **近音词**：`cell` / `sell`，`model` / `module`，`disease` / `decease`。  
- **数字信息**：注意 TED 简介中提到的大规模实验数据，听到数字时先记关键词，再补单位。  
- **学术名词**：`Alzheimer's`, `cancer`, `drug discovery`, `biological experiments`。  
- **转折信号**：重点听 `but`, `however`, `not...but...`, `the challenge is...` 后面的观点。

### 听后复述模板 80-120 词
The talk explains how AI could help scientists understand the complexity of human cells. The speaker suggests that many diseases, such as Alzheimer’s and cancer, remain difficult to treat because researchers still cannot fully predict what is happening inside cells. A possible solution is to build a virtual cell, an AI model trained on large amounts of biological data. This model could help scientists test hypotheses, identify patterns and discover new treatments more efficiently. However, the speaker also implies that such progress depends on reliable data, careful validation and collaboration between biology and computer science.

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### Part 1 问题 1
**Question**：Do you often use AI tools in your daily life?

- **普通答案思路**：说用 AI 查资料、翻译、学习英语。  
- **7 分答案结构**：直接回答 + 具体场景 + 好处 + 小限制。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - I use AI as a learning assistant rather than a complete replacement for thinking.  
  - It helps me save time, but I still need to judge whether the information is reliable.  
  - I mainly use it to brainstorm ideas and polish my language.

### Part 1 问题 2
**Question**：Are people in your country concerned about privacy?

- **普通答案思路**：有人担心，但很多人为了方便会让步。  
- **7 分答案结构**：总体趋势 + 对比不同人群 + 举例。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - People are becoming more privacy-conscious, especially when personal data is involved.  
  - There is often a trade-off between convenience and privacy.  
  - Younger people may be comfortable with technology, but not necessarily with being tracked.

### Part 1 问题 3
**Question**：Do you think technology makes people’s lives safer?

- **普通答案思路**：有时更安全，如监控、导航、医疗；但也有风险。  
- **7 分答案结构**：让步回答 + 双面分析 + 个人判断。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - Technology can improve safety, but it can also create new forms of vulnerability.  
  - It depends on how the technology is governed.  
  - Safety should not come at the cost of basic freedoms.

### Part 2 Cue Card
**Describe a technology that has changed people’s lives.**

You should say:
- what the technology is
- how people use it
- what benefits it brings
- and explain whether you think it has more advantages or disadvantages

- **普通答案思路**：讲 AI，学习、工作、医疗都在用。  
- **7 分答案结构**：  
  1. 引入技术：AI has become part of everyday life.  
  2. 描述用途：study, work, healthcare, translation。  
  3. 分析好处：efficiency, access to information, personalised support。  
  4. 平衡风险：privacy, over-reliance, misinformation。  
  5. 给出判断：beneficial if regulated properly。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - It has moved from a futuristic concept to a practical tool.  
  - The biggest advantage is that it lowers the barrier to knowledge.  
  - The downside is that people may outsource too much of their thinking.  
  - I would say it is beneficial, provided that there are clear ethical boundaries.

### Part 3 深度追问 1
**Question**：Should governments use AI surveillance to prevent crime?

- **普通答案思路**：可以用于严重犯罪，但不能滥用。  
- **7 分答案结构**：限定同意 + 风险 + 监管条件。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - It may be justified in narrowly defined situations.  
  - There must be strict limits on data collection and retention.  
  - Without oversight, crime prevention can easily become social control.

### Part 3 深度追问 2
**Question**：How can society balance security and privacy?

- **普通答案思路**：法律、透明度、公众监督。  
- **7 分答案结构**：承认两者重要 + 提出机制 + 举例说明。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - Security and privacy should not be treated as mutually exclusive.  
  - Any surveillance system should be transparent, accountable and proportionate.  
  - Citizens should know what data is collected and how it is used.

### Part 3 深度追问 3
**Question**：Do you think AI will make scientific research more reliable?

- **普通答案思路**：AI 能处理数据，但也可能出错。  
- **7 分答案结构**：部分同意 + 解释 AI 优势 + 强调人工验证。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - AI can accelerate research by identifying hidden patterns.  
  - However, scientific reliability still depends on evidence and peer review.  
  - AI should be seen as a tool for scientists, not a substitute for scientific judgement.

### Part 3 深度追问 4
**Question**：What skills will people need in an AI-driven society?

- **普通答案思路**：批判性思维、沟通、判断力、学习能力。  
- **7 分答案结构**：列举 2-3 个核心技能 + 解释原因 + 联系未来。  
- **高分表达**：  
  - Critical thinking will become more important, not less.  
  - People need to evaluate information rather than simply consume it.  
  - Human skills such as empathy, communication and ethical judgement will be harder to automate.

### 90 秒口语任务
请你录音或大声回答：

**Do you think AI brings more freedom or more control to modern society?**

要求：
- 先给出明确立场。
- 至少使用 2 个今日表达：`trade-off between convenience and privacy`, `social control`, `transparent and accountable`, `critical thinking`。
- 至少举 1 个现实例子。
- 控制在 90 秒左右。

### 自评标准
- **Fluency**：是否能连续说完 90 秒，中间停顿不超过 3 秒。  
- **Vocabulary**：是否使用了 4 个以上主题词，如 `surveillance`, `privacy`, `AI model`, `ethical boundaries`。  
- **Grammar**：是否至少使用 2 个复杂句，如条件句、让步句、定语从句。  
- **Pronunciation**：`surveillance`, `privacy`, `scientific`, `accountability` 是否发音清楚。  
- **Idea development**：是否有观点、解释、例子和总结，而不是只说零散句子。
