### 长难句拆解

**句子：**  
As the rapid expansion of data centres fuels the AI revolution, environmental groups have raised concerns about their heavy use of electricity and water, while the UN is urging companies to disclose these impacts and switch to renewable power.

**结构拆解：**

- **As the rapid expansion of data centres fuels the AI revolution**：原因 / 背景状语，说明数据中心扩张推动 AI 发展。
- **environmental groups have raised concerns**：主句 1，环保组织提出担忧。
- **about their heavy use of electricity and water**：说明担忧的具体内容。
- **while the UN is urging companies...**：并列对比，说明联合国采取的政策立场。
- **to disclose these impacts and switch to renewable power**：两个并列不定式，表示要求企业做的两件事。

**可模仿表达：**

- As the rapid growth of ______ continues, experts have raised concerns about ______, while governments are urging companies to ______.
- 例句：As the rapid growth of online learning continues, educators have raised concerns about screen fatigue, while schools are urging students to develop healthier study routines.

### 易听错点提醒

- **连读**：`used to hate` 可能听成 /juːstə heɪt/，注意 used to 表示“过去常常”。
- **弱读**：`to`, `and`, `of` 在自然语速里会变轻，不要逐词等重音。

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

### IELTS Speaking Part 1

#### Question 1：Do you usually eat meals with your family?

- **普通答案思路**：说是否经常一起吃饭，原因是时间安排或习惯。
- **7 分答案结构**：直接回答 → 频率 → 解释变化 → 补充感受。
- **高分表达**：
  - We try to have dinner together whenever our schedules allow.
  - It gives us a chance to catch up properly.
  - Family meals are less about the food and more about communication.

**示范答案：**  
Yes, whenever our schedules allow. On weekdays, it can be difficult because everyone is busy with work or study, but we usually try to have dinner together at least a few times a week. For me, family meals are less about the food itself and more about having a proper conversation.

#### Question 2：Were you a fussy eater when you were a child?

- **普通答案思路**：说小时候挑不挑食，举一个例子。
- **7 分答案结构**：过去习惯 → 具体食物 → 现在变化 → 原因。
- **高分表达**：
  - I used to be quite picky about vegetables.
  - My taste has changed a lot over time.
  - I gradually grew out of it.

**示范答案：**  
I used to be quite picky, especially about green vegetables. I remember refusing to eat broccoli or spinach when I was little. But my taste has changed a lot over time, and I gradually grew out of it. Now I actually enjoy food that I used to dislike.

#### Question 3：Do you think people in your country eat healthily?

- **普通答案思路**：有些健康，有些不健康；外卖和快餐增多。
- **7 分答案结构**：总体判断 → 对比年轻人和老年人 → 原因 → 评价。
- **高分表达**：
  - It really depends on lifestyle and income.
  - Convenience often wins over nutrition.
  - There is growing awareness of healthy eating.

**示范答案：**  
It really depends on people’s lifestyle. Older people may still prefer home-cooked meals, which are often healthier, while many young people rely on takeaway food because of their busy schedules. I’d say there is growing awareness of healthy eating, but convenience often wins over nutrition.

### IELTS Speaking Part 2

#### Cue Card

Describe a meal you remember clearly.

You should say:

- when and where you had it
- who you were with
- what you ate
- and explain why this meal was memorable

#### 普通答案思路

可以讲一次家庭聚餐、生日饭、旅行中的饭、学校食堂经历。重点不是菜名，而是情绪和人与人之间的互动。

#### 7 分答案结构

1. **背景**：When / where / who  
2. **细节**：Food + atmosphere  
3. **转折或亮点**：Why it stood out  
4. **总结意义**：What it taught you / why you still remember it

#### 高分表达

- It was not a fancy meal, but it felt incredibly warm.
- What made it memorable was the atmosphere rather than the food.
- It reminded me how powerful simple routines can be.
- Looking back, I think that meal brought everyone closer.

#### 90 秒参考开头

I’d like to talk about a family dinner I had during a winter holiday a few years ago. It was at my grandparents’ home, and almost everyone in my family was there. The food itself was quite simple — mostly home-cooked dishes — but the atmosphere was incredibly warm. What made it memorable was not a particular dish, but the feeling that everyone finally had time to sit down, talk and laugh together.

### IELTS Speaking Part 3

#### Question 1：Why do some children become fussy eaters?

- **普通答案思路**：口味、家庭习惯、父母压力、接触食物少。
- **7 分答案结构**：多因素回答 → 生理原因 → 家庭环境 → 解决方式。
- **高分表达**：
  - Children’s food preferences are shaped by both biology and environment.
  - If parents put too much pressure on them, meals can become a power struggle.
  - Repeated exposure may gradually change their attitude.

#### Question 2：Should schools teach children about healthy eating?

- **普通答案思路**：应该，因为孩子需要知道营养。
- **7 分答案结构**：明确同意 → 学校优势 → 家庭局限 → 实践建议。
- **高分表达**：
  - Schools can play a preventive role.
  - Nutrition education should be practical rather than theoretical.
  - Children are more likely to develop lifelong habits at an early age.

#### Question 3：How has modern life changed family meals?

- **普通答案思路**：更忙，外卖更多，手机影响交流。
- **7 分答案结构**：时间压力 → 技术影响 → 饮食便利化 → 情感后果。
- **高分表达**：
  - Modern life has made meals more convenient but less communal.
  - People often eat separately because their schedules do not overlap.
  - Screens can reduce the quality of face-to-face interaction.

#### Question 4：Do governments have a responsibility to encourage healthier diets?

- **普通答案思路**：有责任，但不能完全控制个人选择。
- **7 分答案结构**：平衡观点 → 政府可做什么 → 个人自由 → 结论。
- **高分表达**：
  - Governments should guide rather than control people’s choices.
  - Public health campaigns can raise awareness.
  - Clear food labelling allows consumers to make informed decisions.
