**长难句拆解**

句子：**The Trump administration also plans to overhaul how the U.S. government funds federal research by supporting more individual scientists and the use of AI, rather than universities.**

结构拆解：

- 主干：**The administration plans to overhaul...**  
  “政府计划改革……”
- 宾语从句：**how the U.S. government funds federal research**  
  “美国政府如何资助联邦科研”
- 方式状语：**by supporting more individual scientists and the use of AI**  
  “通过更多支持个人科学家和 AI 使用”
- 对比结构：**rather than universities**  
  “而不是大学”

逻辑关系：这是一个 **政策变化句**，核心不是“花钱”，而是“改变钱怎么分配”。IELTS 阅读中遇到这种句子，要先抓主干，再看 by 和 rather than 后面的政策手段与对比对象。

可模仿表达：

- **The government plans to overhaul how public money is allocated by prioritising individual researchers rather than large institutions.**
- **Schools can improve how students learn languages by emphasising daily output rather than passive memorisation.**

**易听错点提醒**

- **连读**：  
  “click through all the websites” 中 click through 会连在一起，容易听成一个词。  
  “kind of content” 中 kind of 弱读后接 content。

- **弱读**：  
  “a lot of the internet” 里的 of the 很轻，不要逐词硬听。  
  “what it means for web publishers” 中 it / for 都会弱读。

- **近音词**：  
  **site / sight / cite**：材料中主要是 website/site，注意不要误听成 sight。  
  **add / ad**：讲商业模式时可能出现 ads，容易和 adds 混淆。

- **数字**：  
  注意听 **68%**, **45%**, **75%**, **30%**, **12 or 24 months**。这些数字常用于 IELTS 判断题。

- **人名地名**：  
  **Amanda Natividad**, **Nicolas Bouliane**, **All About Berlin**, **Time magazine**。不必拼写完美，但要能识别谁说了什么。

- **转折信号**：  
  重点听 **but**, **although**, **in the meantime**, **for example**, **right**。这些词后面常出现作者真正观点或解决方案。

**听后复述模板：80-120 词英文**

Today’s episode discusses how AI summaries are changing the way people use the internet. Instead of clicking on websites, many users now get answers directly from search engines or chatbots. This creates a “zero-click” problem because publishers lose traffic, advertising income and affiliate revenue. The episode gives examples of both small websites and major media companies trying to adapt. Some publishers are blocking AI crawlers, making partnership deals, or creating simpler pages for bots. Overall, the episode suggests that AI may improve convenience for users, but it also raises a serious question: who will pay for original online content if fewer people visit the websites that produce it?

## 4. 口语训练：从中式表达到 IELTS 7.0

**Part 1 Question 1: Do you often use AI tools?**

- 普通答案思路：说用不用，举一个学习或工作例子。
- 7 分答案结构：直接回答 + 使用场景 + 优点 + 小限制。
- 高分表达：  
  **I use them mainly as a productivity tool rather than a replacement for my own thinking.**  
  **They are useful for organising ideas, but I still need to verify the information.**

示范答案：  
Yes, I use AI tools quite often, especially when I need to organise ideas or check the structure of a piece of writing. I see them mainly as a productivity tool rather than a replacement for my own thinking. They can save time, but I still need to verify the information because AI-generated answers are not always accurate.

**Part 1 Question 2: Do you prefer getting information from short summaries or full articles?**

- 普通答案思路：短摘要方便，完整文章更可靠。
- 7 分答案结构：偏好 + 情境区分 + 风险意识。
- 高分表达：  
  **It depends on how much depth I need.**  
  **A summary is convenient, but it can oversimplify complex issues.**

示范答案：  
It depends on how much depth I need. If I just want a quick update, a short summary is enough. But when the topic is complex, like health policy or technology regulation, I prefer reading the full article because a summary can oversimplify the issue or leave out important context.

**Part 1 Question 3: How do you usually check whether online information is reliable?**

- 普通答案思路：看来源、日期、多个网站对比。
- 7 分答案结构：来源 + 日期 + 交叉验证 + 谨慎态度。
- 高分表达：  
  **I tend to cross-check information across several credible sources.**  
  **The publication date matters, especially for fast-changing topics.**

示范答案：  
I usually check the source first and then look at the publication date. For fast-changing topics, such as AI or public health, the date matters a lot. I also tend to cross-check information across several credible sources before I believe it or use it in a discussion.

**Part 2 Cue Card**

Describe a technology that has changed the way people get information.

You should say:

- what the technology is
- how people use it
- what advantages it has
- and explain whether you think it has more benefits or drawbacks

**普通答案思路**

可以讲 AI summaries / search engines / short-video platforms。重点说它让信息获取更快，但可能降低深度阅读和判断能力。

**7 分答案结构**

1. 开头明确对象：AI summaries。
2. 描述使用场景：搜索、学习、新闻、工作。
3. 分析好处：节省时间、降低信息门槛。
4. 分析问题：误导、减少点击、影响内容创作者收入。
5. 给平衡结论：有用，但必须配合验证和深度阅读。

**高分表达**

- **It has dramatically lowered the barrier to accessing information.**
- **The downside is that users may become less willing to read beyond the first answer.**
- **It is efficient, but efficiency should not come at the cost of accuracy.**
- **I would describe it as a powerful tool that requires responsible use.**

**Part 3 Question 1: How has AI changed the relationship between users and publishers?**

- 普通答案思路：用户更方便，出版方流量减少。
- 7 分答案结构：用户端收益 + 出版方损失 + 长期影响。
- 高分表达：  
  **AI has shifted attention away from original sources.**  
  **This could weaken the economic foundation of online publishing.**

**Part 3 Question 2: Should AI companies pay publishers for using their content?**

- 普通答案思路：应该，因为内容有成本。
- 7 分答案结构：公平性 + 创作激励 + 执行难度。
- 高分表达：  
  **There should be a fair compensation mechanism.**  
  **Otherwise, high-quality content may become financially unsustainable.**

**Part 3 Question 3: Do you think people are becoming less patient with long-form reading?**

- 普通答案思路：是，短内容多；但深度阅读仍重要。
- 7 分答案结构：承认趋势 + 原因 + 影响 + 例外。
- 高分表达：  
  **Digital platforms have trained people to expect instant answers.**  
  **However, some subjects simply cannot be understood through bite-sized information.**

**Part 3 Question 4: What role should governments play in AI development?**

- 普通答案思路：投资、监管、保护公众。
- 7 分答案结构：支持创新 + 设置规则 + 数据和伦理。
- 高分表达：  
  **Governments should strike a balance between innovation and accountability.**  
  **Public data should be used carefully, especially when it involves health or personal information.**

**90 秒口语任务**

请录音或大声回答：

**Do AI summaries make people better informed, or do they make people less willing to think deeply?**

要求：

- 用英文回答 90 秒。
- 至少使用 3 个表达：  
  **verify the information**  
  **oversimplify complex issues**  
  **the economic foundation of online publishing**
- 结构：观点 15 秒 + 两个理由 50 秒 + 结论 25 秒。

**自评标准**

- **Fluency**：是否能连续说 90 秒，中间停顿是否过多。
- **Vocabulary**：是否用了 topic-specific vocabulary，如 AI summaries, publishers, traffic, reliable sources。
- **Grammar**：是否能混合使用简单句和复杂句，如 although, because, while, rather than。
- **Pronunciation**：关键词是否清楚，尤其是 summary, publisher, reliable, accuracy。
- **Idea development**：是否不仅说“方便”，还分析了准确性、商业模式和长期影响。
